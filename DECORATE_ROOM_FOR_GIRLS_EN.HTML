5 TIPS TO DECORATE A ROOM FOR GIRLS

There is no doubt that decorating youth rooms for girls is a complicated task, since to choose a style, it is necessary to consider age and personality. Therefore, you must choose themes and colors that perfectly suit your tastes, without losing style.
The time comes when girls grow up and you must eliminate the old decoration with dolls, castles, toys, and other elements that no longer work well for them, or that no longer interest them so much.
When it is time to remodel a youth room, it is necessary to consider the personality of the young person, I tell you, I have three daughters, three young girls with personalities and tastes so different that they have not made it easy for me when preparing their rooms.
The important thing about the whole process is to create the space that the girls need, considering their tastes. The older one likes a romantic style in her room, but without going to extremes. The second is the opposite, she doesn't like pink tones or pastels, for her the less details and colors is much better, her style is very minimalist. The minor is more of a Tumblr style, it is a style that has no rules. It only revolves around details that combine and look good with each other; besides, she is a book lover so you can imagine that her focus is to have enough space to place books.
As you will see, I have three great reasons to assure you that it is important to consider the personality and tastes of the girls to create the perfect room for them, that meets their needs and that provides them with that ideal space, in which they love.
Today I give you these 5 tips for youth rooms for girls, I hope you enjoy them and that you can put them into practice to create a beautiful youth room for the princesses of the house.

1.SELECT THE BED

The bed plays a very important role in any room, it is definitely the main element. It is important to make the right choice when it comes to renovating the style of the bedroom. Choosing the most suitable one is essential, not only so that they can have the best of breaks, but also to make good use of the space.

Trundle beds are one of the excellent options for youth bedrooms due to their versatility and durability. In addition, they allow you to take advantage of the space, have an extra mattress, as well as drawers for storage, and only occupy the space of a bed. When needed, the extra bed can be removed and thus convert the room into a double room. Then it is easily collected, and it is again ordered and occupying the minimum space.

If it is for a shared room, an excellent choice for beds is the use of bunk beds. In cases of small apartments, it can be of great help to have such a practical element, since it helps to make the most of the space.

2.SELECTION OF COLORS

White is the favorite when it comes to decorating modern youth bedrooms for girls. It gives it a clean and minimalist look that is much sought after. It is perfect to combine with any color.

If the young woman likes minimalist and clean decoration, with some Nordic or Scandinavian style, the best choice is white and wood. Another of the most sought-after color combinations is white and gray in modern youth bedrooms.

The combination of baby blue and light pink is perfect for feminine youth bedrooms in pastel tones, both on the walls, as well as on the furniture and textiles. If you want to create a more childish or girlish atmosphere, then the combination of pink and white or pink and taupe can be a very flirty option.

Light lilac is a beautiful color and very suitable for youthful and modern bedrooms. As for blue, it is also a beautiful color for youth rooms for girls, both light blue in pastel tones and turquoise in more vivid tones.

Purple or violet is one of the most preferred colors to decorate modern rooms.

One of the best ideas to get it right is to opt for a neutral background and decorate the bedroom with accessories and textiles in bright and striking colors. In this way, changing the accessories will give it a new look.

3.TEXTILES

Textiles are important when decorating youth bedrooms for girls. Adding a rug to the room will give it a warm feeling, visually soften the room and can also be a very interesting design element to play with. Everything will depend on the style you want to give the room.

In the case of curtains or blinds, choose designs and colors that are in tune with the rest of your furniture and textiles.

Another accessory that cannot be missing is cushions. I love cushions because they are the best way to play and change the style of your bedroom. It is always nice to have several on the bed so you can lie down, watch TV or read comfortably. If you have a seat or sofa, you can also add some.

The bedding is also very important for the new decoration, my recommendation is that you keep the bases as smooth or neutral as possible, that is, the sheets and another blanket. This will be your starting point, your canvas to later play with prints and designs on blankets or cushions. Dare to play with the bedroom textiles, combine them, change them, or renew them.

4.SPACIOUS DESK

In a youth room it is very important to have a large and well-lit desk, which is also accompanied by some shelves on the wall to be able to organize books, or what is necessary to be able to fulfill everything related to studies.

The desk is necessary in youth rooms. It is where the girlâ€™s study and it can also be a space for creativity where they can develop their artistic skills, either on the table or on the computer. It is vital to buy a youth desk with a spacious design and space.

5.LIGHTING

Lighting is a very important point to consider, both natural and artificial light are necessary in a youth room. A good idea is lights hanging on the wall to decorate youth bedrooms for girls and give them a special and warm feminine air. Light should be an essential part both to relax and to study.

Another element to consider are the lamps since they can become a striking point in the youth bedroom. Dare to look for original lamps, whether they are lamps with textures, geometric lamps, wicker lamps or any that have a unique design and establish style.

It is also an excellent idea to place an armchair that is consistent with the decoration style. There is a wide variety of armchairs, where their finishes, their manufacturing materials, their upholstery, and their measurements, are completely different and can adapt to any room, style, and decoration.

The shelves are also an essential part when we create the decoration for youthful bedrooms for girls, since they need a lot of space to store their books, their boxes and all their accessories, spaces where they can expose details that they like, such as vases, plants, little boxes, large letters, or whatever they prefer.

Of course, I can give you these ideas and many more so you can decorate a girl's youth room, but in the end, they will have their own ideas. The first step is to ask her what she would like, and with her you can make the best decision.

Do not forget that the room of a teenage girl will be her refuge, her place of rest, study, meeting with friends, leisure, laughter, and tears. For this reason, her bedroom should not have elements that are not to her liking, or that are imposed, since in this way it is possible that the young woman does not feel identified with her bedroom.
