CREATING YOUR READING CORNER AT HOME

Do you enjoy reading? Then I am sure you would love to have your own reading corner at home. Only lovers of reading know how beautiful it is to have a space where they can relax while enjoying a good book.
We are aware that currently there are many people who lead a rhythm of life full of pending tasks and obligations. In most cases it can be stressful. For this reason, it is necessary to dedicate a little time to do what we enjoy.
Remember that everything is about achieving a balance in our lives. That is why we are aware that it is important to fulfill our obligations, but it is also relevant to give ourselves a little time to relax in doing what we love. If reading is what you love, congratulations! You came to the right place because today we will explain how you can create a wonderful reading corner at home.
A corner where you can relax and disconnect from the routine to achieve a balance in your life. One of the greatest benefits is you can see reflected is in your health as well as in many other aspects of your life.
If you do not have much space, do not worry since you only need a comfortable seat, good lighting, and your favorite book. By simply adding some details you can create the ideal atmosphere so that you can relax while reading the book of your choice.
Keep in mind that it is only a space for your rest and personal enjoyment. So be true to your style and your tastes when decorating it.
Pay close attention to the advice that we will give you below so that you can enjoy this pleasant and rewarding hobby much more than you have done so far. Giving yourself the pleasure of decorating a reading corner at home will be one of the best decisions.

1.CHOOSE THE SPOT

The main thing is to select an area of you home that is quiet. If you have a nice view, it would be great, or a place where you simply feel comfortable.

Remember that it does not have to be in the living room. In fact, the current trend allows us to see that you can take advantage of any area in your home that is away from the hustle and bustle of the family, considering that it is a place to relax and focus.

Reading corners are the most appropriate way to take advantage of an unused space in your home and turn it into your refuge to enjoy the pleasure of reading.

Depending on the size of the room or space where your reading corner will be located, it is a good idea to opt for good lamps and furniture so that the space invites you to spend more time there.

2.FURNITURE

A comfortable couch or armchair will be perfect. Keep in mind that it is essential to have a comfortable piece to be able to relax. If you want to prop your feet, a footrest is a plus in comfort.

It would also be an excellent idea to add a small library to the decoration. It does not have to be a huge library. It can be a shelf in any corner, a place that is more than enough to accumulate your favorite books.

You can add a table. The objective is to have a small support surface where you can place your cup of tea or coffee, your things, or the book you have in hand. It will turn this space into something much more practical.

A good idea might be to opt for a shelf with drawers that has enough space both to put your favorite books in and to store other necessities.

3.Key Elements

An excellent idea would be to decorate the space with plain fabrics or with soft prints, warm tones that convey calm, neutrals, grays, or any other color that makes you feel good.

Do not forget the decorative details such as paintings, lamps, and a touch of nature with a plant in one of the corners. Plants often have relaxing properties, reduce anxiety levels, and clear the mind. It is about creating an environment that provides you with calm and well-being.

A spot where you are decorating with books and storing them can be the perfect spot to turn into your reading corner.

A final touch is to light some candles to liven up the place and if you add a relaxing melody, you will create the perfect environment to enjoy your reading.

2.Lighting

Ideally, your reading space should have good natural lighting. But we cannot forget that at some point the day comes to an end and you will need good lighting to be able to enjoy these moments whenever you please.

Also, on a cloudy day you can cry out for an extra dose of natural light all you want but you will need to find a solution. So, you are going to need a functional light that allows you to be just as comfortable reading in rain, thunder, or lightning.

A recommendation would be the use of warm lights with intensity control. For example, a pendant lamp or wall lights with double lighting are perfect for creating calmness.
