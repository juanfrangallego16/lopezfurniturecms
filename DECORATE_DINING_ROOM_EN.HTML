GOOD IDEAS TO DECORATE THE DINING ROOM

Sometimes the dining room becomes the heart of the home, especially when important dates such as Thanksgiving and Christmas holidays are approaching. It is the space in our home where we enjoy delicious meals and interesting conversations with friends and family.
It is said that in a house there are two key points to improve social relations: the dining room and the living room. So, it is normal that it is in these two areas of the home where people invest more efforts when decorating them.
Currently there are a wide variety of styles with which you could decorate the dining room in your home, and if you get creative you can mix some of them.
Possibly you are wondering how to select the correct dining table and it is understandable since the table is the focal point of the dining room. If you want a specific style, look for tables that perfectly suit it, but do not forget to consider your tastes. At the end of it all, it is a space in your home that you will enjoy with your family and friends.
When selecting the table for the dining room it is necessary that you consider the available space. This point is relevant so that you can create a comfortable and welcoming environment.
Good lighting is also necessary for this space in the home. Pendant lamps are perfect for this area. One of the most important aspects is to maintain the symmetry between the size of the table and the lamp you choose, so that your environment does not look disproportionate.
Also consider the accessories that will complement the decoration of your dining room, such as a work of art, a mirror, or shelves with decor. The colors and materials you use will help to unify, give it visual balance, and harmony.
Today I want to share with you some ideas that can be of inspiration to decorate the dining room, whether it needs a complete makeover or just some basic design improvements, such as a new coat of paint or a new dining room set. The possibilities are infinite.

1.YOU CAN OPT FOR A MODERN DINING ROOM

A modern dining room doesn't have to look stark and cold. You can use colors, beautiful furniture, and accessories to add character and interest. In this space you will enjoy quality family time, therefore, my recommendation is that it be clutter free and full of style.

The modern dining room is elegant, but simple at the same time. With character, but quite simple and minimalist. In this style, the simplicity of the straight lines in the furniture is sought. A plus in a modern dining room would be that you add something unique that you hardly find in another dining room. Think of an original idea and carry it out.

2.BE INSPIRED BY ART FOR THE DINING ROOM

I am very sure that candles are another complement that never fails in any type of decoration. For this holiday you can put together beautiful arrangements accompanying the candles like pumpkins and pineapples. Always try to do it within the autumn hue and creating something cute and original. But I can assure, that not adding candles to your decor will show.

You can even decorate an arrangement with pumpkins, flowers in autumn tones or dried flowers and some candles. I can assure you that with a little creativity you will create some beautiful arrangements.

3.CLASSIC DINING ROOM DOES NOT GO OUT OF FASHION

A classic dining room is elegant and formal. Furniture is solid wood with curvy details and hand carvings. In this style you will find golden tones that can be reflected in mirror frames, works of art, or decorative lamps.

A Persian or oriental rug will always be a good complement that will help add elegance to this space in your home. Ceiling and wall moldings are also common in decorations with a classic style.

Curtains for a classic dining room can include multiple layers. And the final touch would be a beautiful lamp that fits perfectly in this wonderful style.

4.VINTAGE STYLE DINING ROOM

A good alternative to decorate the dining room is to add some vintage pieces and decor. These old details will always be a topic of conversation.

You could include antique tea pieces, metal pitchers, and small glass bottles that work well as vases.

This style is often partnered with one or more key pieces of 20th century design, vintage dining rooms combine styles, colors, and materials from other times to become a harmonious and very personal space. This style mixes elements from previous decades and the contemporary era to compose a unique dining room.

Pick whichever combination you like best, or just use a neutral scheme, like gray and white. Walls with patterned tapestries can be an excellent touch. Then choose your furniture, glass tables, or rustic woods. Add colorful fabrics, such as curtains, rugs, among others. And some accessories like chandeliers, lamps, and pieces of art.

5.MINIMALIST DINING ROOM

The minimalist decor is simple, elegant, and uncluttered. Each thing has a specific place and function. I recommend selecting a neutral color palette, you can also add few decorative accessories. Make sure you have good lighting and that the furniture selected for the dining room is linear and at right angles, these are some of the characteristics to achieve a minimalist dining room.

The minimalist style has been imposed over the years. This type of decoration is characterized using neutral tones, by its smooth finishes and simple lines, by its spaciousness and by being a very useful, comfortable, and practical style.

The decoration of the minimalist dining room is very simple since hardly any accessories are needed. In addition, it is a perfect style for small dining rooms.

I hope that these good ideas to decorate your dining room are of great help and that you can enjoy wonderful meals in the company of your family and friends.
