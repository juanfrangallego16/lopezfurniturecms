5 TIPS TO DECORATE THE HOUSE OF YOUR DREAMS

We all know that decoration is a matter of taste. But it is also good to keep in mind some tricks for this wonderful task.

That is the reason why in this article we want to share with you 5 tips to decorate your home.

What a great emotion you feel when it comes time to give your home personality and style!

And it is that we can not deny that this can generate some concern.

But keep calm! We are here to help you.

Currently there are so many options available and there are many decisions that must be made when choosing furniture or items for each space in the home.

That is why I can assure you that these 5 tips to decorate your home will be of great help so that you can make the best decisions.

We started!

1.DEFINE YOUR BUDGET.

Being clear about how much you can spend to decorate your house is very important, since, if your budget does not allow you to decorate it completely, you can establish priorities and in this way, you will give life to each space of your home without this taking away the dream.

When preparing the budget, do not forget to include even the smallest detail that you will need to create the spaces that you want so many.

The budget must be adjusted to the needs and priorities of each space, in this way you will be aware of whether the decoration will have to be done in stages and thus your project will be much more realistic and achievable.

The most important thing is that you and your family can enjoy each of the spaces.

You will see how this dream can make it come true!

2.Furniture Selection

Now is the time to have fun! In the entire process of decorating this is what we enjoy the most.

Remember to take into account the size of each space in your home. In this way, you select the best furniture for your space.

Choosing furniture can be difficult for those who have not defined the style they want to in the different spaces. If this is your case, we recommend you seek guidance from experts in or look at references online to help you define your desired style.

If you already know the style you want, it will be much easier for you to select the furniture that will make your space a home.

3.Choose the color palette

To make the right selection of the color palette for your home, we recommend that you first select the furniture. Why you might ask.

If you choose the colors for the walls first, you might face a very common issue when it comes time to select your furniture. Let’s just say that the furniture you want is not available anytime soon. You will be forced to choose an entirely new set that fits the space in your home.

It is for this reason that we recommend choosing the furniture first and from there you define which color palette goes with the style you have selected.

One point to consider when selecting the colors is that they convey feelings & emotions. This is good to consider for all the spaces in your home, but especially in the bedrooms to achieve a feeling of harmony and relaxation. This will ensure you rest well.

If you are interested in more information about how colors contribute to your home, let us know. We might write a blog just for you.

4.Lighting is very important.

Good lighting creates the feeling of larger and more pleasant spaces. This is one of the most important points when decorating your home.

If the entry of natural light is relevant, so is choosing artificial lighting. Making a good selection of lamps & pendant lights that harmonize with each space in your home is a key point.

To make a good lighting selection, you must know the height of the ceiling in your home. This will help you if you want to add pendant lights. They come in many different sizes.

Adding light in a strategic place in the house can visually add meters of amplitude to space.

Besides, light influences our mood so it is important to decorate every room with good lighting, following different guidelines in each of the spaces.

The same thing happens with lighting as with the color of our walls. Both aspects must be combined to favor the atmosphere that is created in our home. These details will decide if the home feels nice and cozy, just like you want it.

5.Don't forget the details.

Home decor accessories are necessary to give style and life to each space. A good selection of pillows, fabrics, rugs, mirrors, pictures, centerpieces, and other decorations that add that personal touch to your home cannot be missed.

These will give you that wonderful personal touch that characterizes you.

Last but not least, do not forget to have fun at each stage of the decorating process. Remember… this is your space. Create it with love, joy, and passion.

If you apply these tips, congrats to you! We are sure that you will have many happy days in your home.
