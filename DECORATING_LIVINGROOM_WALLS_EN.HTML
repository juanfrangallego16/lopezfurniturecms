IDEAS FOR DECORATING THE WALLS IN THE LIVING ROOM

It is true that when we prepare to decorate the living room of our home we think mainly about the furniture, but we cannot forget the walls. The walls in your spaces have great relevance to achieve the decoration you want in a very effective way and much more when it comes to the walls of the living room.
For many, wall decoration is an art and for this reason it has many advantages since new ideas are always emerging. So, when you want to give the living room a change, you can always start with the walls.
Decorating the walls in an ingenious way can be the best way to make a style statement in the living room and in any space in the house, since accents can be created in them, by incorporating a painting, an art abstract, or simply by adding eye-catching wallpaper.
Today I want to share some ideas so that you can decorate the walls of your living room with a lot of style.

1.PAINT A FOCAL WALL

I'm one of those people who think that new paint can do wonders. If you decide to paint the walls in your home, I recommend choosing a tone that stands out to paint a focal wall in the living room, it is a very simple resource, but I am sure it will give you great results.

There are those who choose to highlight the wall behind the largest sofa, since in this way they make the furniture stand out in the living room. Especially if dark tones are chosen for the wall and light tones for the furniture.

2.DARE TO COVER YOUR WALLS

A wonderful way to add design to your walls quickly is by placing a wallpaper that you like and that fits perfectly with your decoration. As in the previous point you can create a focal wall, or place it on all walls, for maximum effect.

In addition to wallpaper, vinyl, and wood accents, there are other types of self-adhesive textured coatings to decorate your home. You can find siding sheets that look like brick or even stone. These plates are glued on the wall directly and generate a very original effect both visually and to the touch. With this idea you can achieve those white brick walls that you like so much.

With any of these options you will achieve a spectacular view, and I assure you that it will be much more intense if you only apply some of these options on a focal wall.

There is no doubt that wood is a material that cannot lack in your decor. It can be combined with warm or cold colors and is suitable for all styles of decoration. Therefore, it is a good ally. You just must choose the right wood for the style that suits you best.

3.ADD MOLDING TO THE WALL

Wall molding can instantly elevate the decorative style and you can be sure that it is a great way to keep the walls of your living room blank and add some relief to it that will add a touch of interest.

There is no doubt that adding moldings to the walls is an excellent option to give your living room a classic and vintage look. These elegant finishes have proven to be more than just a way to hide a damaged wall. Dare to decorate the walls with moldings and I am sure that you will love the result.

4.PLACE A PICTURE ON THE WALL

I can say that this is one of my favorite options. Selecting a good size painting that stands out in the middle of the decoration is an excellent idea to decorate your living room with.

If you have several works that you want to exhibit, you can create a small gallery on one of the walls. In this case, my recommendation would be that you consider the relationship of the colors between the works of art and the rest of the decoration.

If you are a fan of symmetry, a gallery with paintings of the same size and some color as a focal wall may be your best option. It will be even better if the paintings are related in some way. If they are all a part of a series, it would be wonderful. You will see that it will a good conversation starter.

5.DISPLAY YOUR PHOTOS ON THE WALLS

You could select some photos of special moments and use them to decorate the walls of your living room. A good idea is to hang large photographs on the wall, unifying them in some way. Even if you prefer, you could choose to put black and white photos.

There is no doubt that we all love photographs, especially when we have accumulated many that transport us to happy times, to special occasions that we always love to remember.

It is a good idea if you want to keep in mind beautiful moments that you have lived intensely. You could even use photographs of your family, of a friend, of places you like, of so many things, with which you could take a beautiful photo collage that can be used to decorate the wall with originality and style.

6.PLACE SHELVES TO DECORATE

You can also choose to place pictures and decorative elements on the shelves to create a much cooler and more serene space. There you can put your favorite objects, so that they are always in view.

If you add shelves, you can create a special place for your books. The size of the furniture will depend on the space available and the number of books you need to organize.

Don't let your precious collection get dusty in a box, bring it to light! You can use your collection to decorate the walls of your living room and have it in view of everyone, so that others can also enjoy your precious objects.

7.DECORATE YOUR WALLS WITH WICKER BASKETS

If you are thinking of an original and natural-looking wall decoration, nothing better than to resort to wicker. Above all, if you have already assembled a decoration that includes rustic furniture, decorating a wall with wicker baskets will be a fabulous success.

Wicker is strong and has wonderful natural hues, which lend itself beautifully to decoration. It is placed in patio and garden settings.

In this wall decoration with baskets, they are used in beautiful tones, sizes, and designs that will give the room a different touch.
