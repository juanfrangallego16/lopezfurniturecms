IDEAS FOR DECORATING YOUR SMALL GARDEN

Who wouldn’t like to have even a small garden at home? Having a beautiful space to enjoy the outdoors is wonderful. Being able to enjoy the sunny afternoons in the summer, as well as enjoying the cold winter afternoons with a delicious cup of coffee and in the company of our loved ones, are pleasures that are priceless.
If you have a small garden at home, you should think about taking advantage of it and transforming it into a place you fall in love with. I am sure that with a little creativity and ingenuity you can create a beautiful and functional space, and of course it is worth asking for the help of experts in the field, if you prefer.
Something that you should keep in mind when decorating a small garden is to be able to adapt it to your needs, without losing style of course.
My goal with this article is that you can make the most of your garden, enjoying wonderful moments in the comfort of your home, and in the same way being able to connect in some way with nature.
I am sure that by applying some of these ideas you will be able to transform your small garden into an oasis made for rest and family enjoyment. Let’s not wait any longer and start transforming it into your favorite place in your home.

1.COMBINE DIFFERENT GROUND MATERIALS

An excellent option to create the feeling that your small garden is a little bigger is to use different materials on the ground to determine zones and functions. Dare to choose combinations between grass, wood, stone, tiles, among other materials that will add style to your garden and help you define the spaces within it.

In the case of grass, I must point out that it is valid to use natural and artificial grass. Both can be used to create interesting designs in your garden. In my case I must say that I prefer natural grass. I think and feel that it makes you feel more in tune with nature. This is a great idea to integrate the green of the plants in the ground.

2.CREATE DIFFERENT SPACES WITHIN THE GARDEN

With the ground modifications, you can also create some divisions with plants and furniture. We can create different spaces within the same garden. It's just a matter of knowing how to organize it.

In fact, you can make use of furniture in different materials to mark the differences from one area to another within the garden. An excellent idea for small spaces is to take advantage of height. By decorating the walls of your garden, you create the feeling that it is a higher space. There is no doubt that installing a vertical garden will help you integrate nature without taking up much space on the ground.

3.TAKE ADVANTAGE OF THE SPACE WITH CUSTOMIZED AND LIGHT FURNITURE

In small spaces, it is advisable to opt for lighter color tones and pieces that provide visual cleanliness. You can also opt for foldable-i furniture that allows you to quickly transform your space. You can look for furniture that can be easily moved to use in different areas of the garden.

To make the most of every square inch, custom furniture is the best option. It does not need to be very complex; it can be simple. With the help of some nice cushions, you can create a beautiful and comfortable environment to enjoy your garden.

4.CONTEMPORARY

For a small garden it is advisable to select plants that are not too large as these can create the feeling of smaller spaces. Choose and position the plants strategically in the space, combining different sizes to create a more interesting design.

A good idea to decorate with plants, especially in narrow gardens, is to place them on the edges of this space and leave the center free. Installing the same type of plant organized in a linear way gives your garden a more organized appearance.

You could also add a fountain. There are different types that can be an incredible piece in your outdoor space and will give your garden a very relaxed and Zen atmosphere.

5.YOU CAN USE SIMPLE LINES IN FURNITURE

Opt for furniture with simple and straight lines. This will help you create the feeling of order and harmony that are necessary in a small garden.

As I have said in previous articles, in small spaces less is more. By not overloading your garden, it will look much more spacious, serene, and pleasant.

Place the necessary pieces of furniture and plants, to create a charming oasis that you and your family can enjoy.

6.CHOOSE A SOFT COLOR PALETTE

As I have said before, lighter tones create a feeling of spaciousness and bring light to the space. It is relevant to considered this for small spaces.

In addition, it is true that the green of the plants contrasts perfectly with lighter colors and of course, if you want you can also add a more intense tone in decorative details such as cushions or pots, among others.

7.DON'T FORGET TO ORGANIZE AND MEASURE THE SPACES

Unquestionably, every inch counts in small spaces. That is why it is advisable to carefully measure all the furniture and of course, leave space for passage so that there is a flow when walking around your garden.

Do not rush and think carefully about each piece and the plants that will help you create a wonderful, cozy, and functional garden in which you will want to spend quality moments in the company of your family. Don't think twice and get the most out of your small garden. I hope that you have found this information helpful. If you enjoyed this article, please share it with friends and family.
