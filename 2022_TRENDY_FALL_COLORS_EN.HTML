TRENDY COLORS FOR WALLS IN 2022

It is true that the colors that are chosen for the decoration of a home say a lot about the personality of those who inhabit it. There is a wide variety of shades from which you can choose the ones that perfectly match your personality and style.
We know that white is one of the most popular because it brings light to the spaces where it is used, but there are also other colors that can be adapted to achieve a much more welcoming and stylish space.
Each color provides a different sensation, so it should be chosen considering our personal style, but also the space in which we will apply it. Keep in mind that painting a living room is not the same as a kitchen, neither is it the same to paint a narrow corridor than a large dining room.
Dare to choose a new color for any of the spaces in your home. It is possible that after seeing some of the colors that are in trend this year you will want to paint your entire home again or at least some spaces that you want to highlight.
When choosing the color for each wall, you must consider various aspects of the room and its function to find the ideal tone.
Light colors are ideal for small, dimly lit spaces. While warm tones are ideal for creating cozy spaces and more saturated tones are great for impact and style. Define the needs of the space and your personal tastes to find the ideal color for each environment in your home.
Today I bring you some colors that are in trend for this 2022 and their different applications in home spaces, considering their properties. Beautiful colors that will give your home style and personality.

1.ARE YOU LOOKING FOR A COZY GRAY - HYGGE GREY

This tone is widely used in spaces that you want to give personality to with a neutral tone, plus it can be perfectly combined with other colors.

This shade is a very soft grey, with warm notes making it perfect for bedroom and hallway walls.

The term Hygge is of Danish origin and refers to the comfort of home that this color provides. It is a color that generates well-being and serenity and, being a light tone, brings light to any space, making it feel bigger and fresher.

Hygge is a very peculiar word that has always been and will always be rooted in the Scandinavian style. It has no direct translation; although, it is frequently described as warmth.

2.DO YOU PREFER A GRAY WITH PERSONALITY - CAUSEWAY

This gray tone is trendy. It is true that this color has stronger and more saturated notes, but there is no doubt that it brings elegance and style to any space in the home. It's ideal for large spaces, but it's also perfect for creating focal walls in living rooms or dining rooms.

It is an imposing color, but also neutral, which can be adapted to any decorative style. Its name refers to the basalt columns in Giant's Causeway, in Ireland, relating to its imposition and strength.

3.HOW ABOUT AN ELEGANT BLUE - NAVY BLUE

There is no doubt that the blue color for interiors has been in trend for years and it seems that it will continue to do so for a long time. It is a very stylish color that brings elegance and style to any space, it is related to the sea and the Mediterranean style.

There are even those who use it in bedroom decor. It is a deep color, with a lot of personality.

It is generally combined with other lighter colors to enhance its hue and is considered a classic in interior design.

4.DO YOU PREFER A SERENE COLOR - GLASSY BLUE

If you want to decorate your home with blue tones but also bring light to the spaces in your home, you can opt for this tone. As you can see in the image, it is a very soft blue that will give your spaces harmony and tranquility.

It is perfect when applying it in small spaces. It will help create a feeling of spaciousness in the room and it will give light & a beautiful style to your home.

5.DO YOU LIKE THE FRESHNESS OF GREEN - SUCCULENT GREEN

Green, like blue, is another color that is related to nature and has been trending in home interiors for some time because it is a fresh color.

This tone is a soft green, capable of bringing a certain warmth to the home. Ideal for living rooms or dining rooms.

This shade is inspired by the color of succulents and looks great combined with the different shades of green found in houseplants. An ideal color for nature lovers and to bring the outside in.

6.THE VITALITY OF GREEN - SAGE GREEN

This beautiful tone is inspired by the green color of sage leaves and of course, as you have already noticed, it is darker than the previous one. But in the same way it maintains the freshness that characterizes this color.

This tone brings a lot of personality to any space in the home, but it is also a timeless color, capable of adapting to different decoration styles.

7.A BEAUTIFUL EMERALD GREEN - MALACHITE

Related to the famous jewel tones, this green is a very dark, deep, and indisputably elegant tone that will give any space a lot of style and attention. It is based on the green of malachite, so it has a lot of character; but also, despite being a strong color, it can bring harmony to your spaces.

It is a very strong color ideally for youth rooms because it has a lot of personality, but also gives a sense of calm and balance.

8.IF YOU ARE LOOKING FOR A ROMANTIC TONE - ROSE BREEZE

If you are one of those who love soft bright colors that also add style to the home, let me tell you that this pink tone is ideal for you.

Soft pink tones add warmth and light to the space in which you apply them. In addition, this tone is loaded with very beautiful notes of romanticism which are very pleasant.

This tone is perfect for the living room or for modern and youthful rooms. It is a tone characterized by its luminosity and its delicacy. It creates a very cozy atmosphere in the home.

9.A BEAUTIFUL PINK - CANYON

This pink is a shade with a lot of personality and more depth than the previous one. This color is ideal to integrate all the strength of pink, but with a much softer and more elegant note.

This shade of pink is also widely used in living rooms, dining rooms, and bedrooms. It is a color that goes very well with vintage, modern, and eclectic decoration styles.

I hope this article brings you enough understanding and helps add personality to your walls.
