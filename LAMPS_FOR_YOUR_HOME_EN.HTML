HOW TO CHOOSE LAMPS FOR YOUR HOME

Having good lighting at home is important, since this has effects on our life inside the home. Having good natural lighting is the best thing that can happen to your home. Without a doubt, it is very important for your home to be well lit during the day, illuminated at night. We cannot deny that lamps are important and a great decorative piece that can give the perfect touch to your decor.
I think the biggest conflict you can have is making the right decision for each space in the home. However, this is not a difficult task to accomplish.
It is important that you are aware of the spaces where you will place the lamps. There are key points that can guide you to achieve the desired goal.
For example, determine what spaces require more lighting. Pay attention to the size of the lamps, considering the space in which they will be. In the case of floor or table lamps, it is important to consider that the area in which they will be located has plugs nearby. Considering all these points, it is time to choose the type of lamp or lamps for your home.
There is a wide variety of lamps for all tastes and for all styles. From the most extravagant to the most simple and straightforward. Now, how to select the perfect lamp and not die trying? Here are some ideas that will help you make the best decision.

1.PENDANT LIGHTS

For many, ceiling lamps are one of the most attractive options from an aesthetic point of view. Whether you decide on recessed lighting or pendant lights, it will undoubtedly be very important to consider the style of decoration you have at home.
You can find great classic lamps such as chandeliers, lamps with metal structures that fit perfectly with urban and industrial styles, among others. Or simpler lamps that can serve both in a Nordic environment and in a minimalist space.
Keep in mind that not all lamps have to be the focus of the room, so you can choose a lamp that provides supporting light or simply serves a decorative function.

2.TABLE LAMPS

These lamps are perfect to illuminate some spaces, in the living room, in the bedroom, on a table in our reading corner, among others. You can find a wide variety of designs that can complement your decoration well.
They are perfect as an additional light source and help create illuminated spaces to carry out daily tasks and fulfill peaceful desires.
Table lamps can have different functions, each of them meets specific needs and it is important to know what you want to achieve in order to choose the right lamp for your home.

3.FLOOR LAMPS

We can say that the intention of these lamps is like that of the previous ones but with more illuminated presence. To choose the right floor lamp it is not enough to look at the design. The space where you place it, and its function are decisive.
Due to their height, they are the best option to create positive environments. In the living room you will achieve a warm climate with a floor lamp and a fabric shade. Its light is important, but so is its design. Choose one that fits with the decoration in your home.
This type of lamp can become your perfect company in the reading corner. Keep in mind that if the color of the lamp shade is darker, it can darken the space located around the light beam.
There are floor lamps that have such a beautiful and slender shape that they become almost sculptural pieces that deserve a preferential space so that all eyes fall on them.

4.WALL LAMPS

These types of lamps turn out to be discreet and can offer as much light as a ceiling lamp would. Of course, the effect is totally different because what is illuminated is the perimeter. They are perfect to be placed in a large room, but they are also an excellent option for a corridor or even on a terrace.
They work perfectly as complementary lights, although there are models that can give as much light as a ceiling lamp. Defining the amount of light you want in that space, will help determine what wall lamp is best for your space.
It is said that "the union is strength" I dare to say that it is also like that in terms of lighting. If your goal is to have a well-lit home, a good idea would be to combine several of the lighting options mentioned above. For example, standard sized rooms can be favored with three different light sources. However, larger rooms may require more points, while smaller rooms may not need more than one or two.
Do not forget to also pay attention to the intensity of the light. It can be interesting to combine soft and strong lights. Always have a main focal point and let the rest be simply a secondary light to carry out specific activities.
There are lamps that fit perfectly into any style of decoration. I must mention that this happens especially with the simplest lamps. But if, for example, you have decoration with a very marked style such as a rustic style, it may be better to go for a simpler or even classic lamp instead of selecting a modern style.
If you need inspiration, I advise you to look for ideas on our website or in home decor magazines. Always remember that regardless of the lamps you choose, they must be in harmony with the environment they are a part of.
When choosing the lamps that will illuminate your spaces you have two main focuses, to choose lamps that attract attention or lamps that will mix in with the rest of the decor. In both cases, the tone of light must be adequate, considering the activities that you will carry out in the space, so that your lamp can create the right atmosphere.
I hope this article will serve as a guide for you to find the right lamps for your home. Thank you very much for reading and I say goodbye until the next article.
