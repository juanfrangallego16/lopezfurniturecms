NEED FURNITURE TO ENJOY BREAKFAST IN THE KITCHEN?

Organizing and setting up furniture and accessories for a cute breakfast area is a fun idea that will provide you with relaxed stress-free mornings, most of the time. How big or how small your kitchen is does not matter because there is a solution for everything and several options to choose from when selecting furniture and accessories for your kitchen.
Some options are to place a breakfast bar or even extra counter space where you can have breakfast, lunch, or dinner informally.
In this article, I want to mention some of my favorite kitchen furniture options and I am hoping to inspire a fresh idea.

1.EVERYTHING IN ONE PLACE

One of the best ways to enjoy a delicious, healthy, and calm breakfast every morning is to have everything you need at your fingertips. For this, the best option is to make or buy more storage space specifically for everything you need for your best mornings. From a microwave to a coffee maker, toaster, mugs, tea bags, etc. You know what you need and if it can be in one space well organized, your mornings would be easier.

I think the idea of a breakfast cabinet is fantastic! In this cabinet, we have everything we need and it is placed in a space with plugs to be able to connect electric appliances like a microwave. It is a very interesting option and perfect for small kitchens.

2.FOLDING OR EXPANDING TABLES

This is an option that fits perfectly in small kitchens and allows you to save space, while still adding your touch with chairs or stools of your choice. Your style always matters! Give the kitchen the style you’ve always wanted. Make it perfectly comfortable and pleasing to you.

Folding or expanding tables are the best allies for small spaces. When you’re done using them, they don't take up as much space. When it's time to use them, they are extremely functional and they can hold higher capacities.

3.HOW ABOUT A SMALL SIDEBAR?

This idea is also perfect for small kitchens since space is limited. You can choose to place a small bar on the kitchen wall if you have a wall or a portion of the wall free to use.

If you look closely, this option is quite interesting and at the same time very decorative. Also, with the rights stools, you can enjoy a small and comfortable but informal space.

In my opinion, the wooden bar would be beautiful; although, it would also look spectacular in natural stone, or the same material used for the kitchen countertop.

If your kitchen is larger or is open to the living room, you could create a bar as an extra surface or even as a visual division of space.

4.HOW ABOUT AN ISLAND?

Well-designed islands can be transformed into an integral part of kitchens. They are usually positioned in the center of the kitchen and provide extra space for cooking and eating as well. They can also be the focal point of the kitchen. You can use the overlapping portion of the island as breakfast seating.

Kitchen islands not only add more space for dining, cooking, and entertaining, but they can also function perfectly as a central design piece.

5.TABLE IN THE KITCHEN

A small table in the kitchen is also a good option. If the space is quite small, a round table would be the best alternative.

There are also corner dining tables that take advantage of the empty corners. This does require available square footage, so you don’t crowd your kitchen.

Some homes have kitchens with an empty space leading you into the living room. A common use of the space is placing a dining table as a transition element.


To conclude, I must mention that one of my most important keys to maintaining order in any space is to classify the elements. It should be noted that this also works in the space intended for breakfast.

If you have several shelves to organize, you could try separating things based on their use.

You can always reserve a space for appliances such as coffee maker, toaster, juicer, among others.

In another space you can place the cups, plates, bowls, cutlery, and more. And of course, another one for the containers in which you put the coffee, the sweeteners, the cereals, the cocoa, and the tea.

The important thing is that you can enjoy your breakfasts in harmony and that the space you have selected for your mornings adapts perfectly to you and your style.

I hope that these ideas are to your liking and that you find some or all of these options interesting. Thank you very much for reading, I say goodbye once again until the next article.
