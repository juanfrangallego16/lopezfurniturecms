SOME FURNITURE DOESN'T GO OUT OF STYLE

Furniture is classified as timeless when its shape, beauty, and essence remains exact over the years. What I mean by this is that there are pieces that have indisputably stood the test of time and continue to be loved by new generations, regardless of whether they are eye-catching for their form and functionality or simply because they add a nice look to any room.

If you are the type of person who makes sure your furniture looks good in your home and you also look at current styles despite the passage of time, I encourage you to opt for classic designs, materials, and colors. If you want to be a little more daring, go ahead and add some pieces that give that unique and personal touch to your decoration.

From my point of view, when we talk about timeless furniture, we refer to basic furniture that fits perfectly into any style and survives the test of time. In fact, I can say that there are pieces that are revalued over time and always add value to any space in your home.

Is timeless furniture a safe investment? Yes. Although trends change over time, they are elements that give continuity and help us to have decorative stability in the home.

In this article, I will mention some furniture that does not go out of style. In addition, will be a total success in your decoration and for this they should be at the top of your list. There is furniture that, even if time passes, it will always look good.

1. CHESTERFIELD SOFA

I understand this style of sofas never goes out of style and I dare to assure you that it will last for a long time. Since the Viscount of Chesterfield commissioned a cabinetmaker to make this armchair in the 18th century, the elegant armchair, with its classic character, English sobriety, and exquisite design, is much more than an armchair. I think it has become an icon that is always a success for those who decide to add it to their living room or office. There is no doubt that it is one of the longest-lived pieces of furniture.

In this style, the most common model is upholstered in aged brown leather, but today there are many different finishes available, making this sofa a perfect piece for almost any interior decoration style.

For me it is a sofa with personality and a piece that also adds a touch of glamor to any room, especially those that are upholstered in velvet. I think it would not be an exaggeration to say that having a Chesterfield is having a treasure that stands the test of time. In fact, the older they are, the more beautiful they are.

2. WHITE SOFA

There is no sofa that survives more trends than white. Regardless of its style, if you choose a white sofa, you can be sure that it will never go out of style. In addition, it provides light and never looks ginormous in the space it occupies.

Not only will it be the star of the room that captures all eyes, but its fits into any style and yes, it is timeless. It can adapt to any decorative trend; in a few words it is basic decoration. To keep it spotless, it is advisable to choose anti-stain fabrics or choose from the new selection of stainless fabrics.

3. A CONSOLE IN WOOD AND IRON

These are Tables that fit in any corner of the home, from the hall to a hallway. You can find some that turn out to be practical and light. Those that are made of wood and iron, with straight lines, have become a modern classic that fits into many styles of decoration.

I have no doubt that they are the ideal support surface for small and especially narrow spaces, such as corridors or halls. In addition, they are very easy to integrate into the decoration of the house, which is why it is a piece that has stood the test of time as a wild card that turns out to be very useful.

4. EAMES CHAIR

They come in all colors and are perfect for combining with classic furniture, which is why they have become a piece that has endured over the years. Charles and Ray Eames created the DSR in the 50s as a low-cost chair suitable for all kinds of spaces.

The concept of these chairs was clear, having a simple structure that could accommodate different variants in its design. The result also seems obvious, this chair has become an iconic piece of furniture, recognized, and used in interior decoration very frequently.

5. WOODEN COFFEE TABLE

Although time passes, the wooden coffee table will continue to be a practical, decorative piece that always works in many styles of decoration. No matter what colors you decorate the room with, a wooden table will be perfect. From my point of view, the coffee table should fulfill a double function: to give style and to be practical.

Unquestionably, choosing a wooden coffee table that attracts attention is the key to adding style to your home decoration.

6. TUFTED HEADBOARDS

This type of upholstery is very famous and consists of creating padded cells through sunken buttons. This type of upholstery has a long history and has been used for both armchairs, chairs, trunks, headboards, among others.

Tufted headboards never go out of style, although in certain pieces of furniture they are reserved for more classic styles. In the case of headboards, it is much more transversal, there are modern, romantic, and classic tufted headboards. In short, I think it is a plus to your decor.

Would you like to decorate your home following the latest trend in interior decoration? I think that anyone would love to, but I am aware that not all budgets allow you to follow up with the latest furniture trends in interior design. However, a viable option could be to keep the house on-trend by changing just a few accessories, which are affordable pieces, yet turn the look of home decor on its head. What do I mean? I mean your cushions, the curtains, the lamps, the rugs and those decorative details such as wall art, or mirrors. You can now add some eternal pieces like the ones I just mentioned and add a new trend that you like or even make changes with each season. It is a financial solution, and it will be very effective.

Remember there are pieces that not only stand the test of time, but with each passing year, their popularity rises. Invest in them and you will have a long-lasting beautiful home. Thank you very much for reading and I say goodbye until the next article.
