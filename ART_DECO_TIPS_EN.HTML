ART DECO DECORATION TIPS

Art Deco in interior design is undoubtedly a style that broke everything previously established and came to fill the spaces with luxury and elegance.
Did you know that Art Deco can adapt all the glamor of the 20s to the 21st century? This style reached its height of popularity in the 20's, 30's, and 40's. It is the style of glamour, luxury, and symmetrical designs with exuberant shapes.
In this style you can see the use of exotic woods, some geometric motifs, and the high gloss finishes that are so characteristic of Art Deco in interior design. Even if you look closely, you can see that Art Deco has some hints of French, Egyptian, and Mediterranean influences.
Generally, Art Deco style spaces have wall coverings and accessories in all their splendor. Regardless of whether you include Art Deco in all the decoration of your home or simply want to add some details of this style to your decoration, you can be sure that this style will embellish your spaces.
If you are attracted to the Art Deco style for decorating your home or a particular space, pay attention to the tips that I bring to you in this article. So, let’s get to work because the time has come to fill your home with emblematic details loaded with the Art Deco style.

1.LET'S TAKE A COLORS TOUR

Art Deco tried to emulate the drama and grandeur of the big movie productions. Color palettes in neutral tones were used that were perfectly combined with bright fabrics and very luxurious materials.

It is important to note that it is a style that integrates colors with geometric shapes and ornamental details. Some of the most used colors are white, black, red, green, pale blue, and silver. But the color that cannot be absent because it is very characteristic of this style is the color gold. Yes, it is true that sometimes it is a bit complex to use it without overloading the space.

Currently, many companies manufacture furniture and accessories that make it relatively easy to have a home inspired by Art Deco decoration.

2.LET'S REVIEW THE MATERIALS

Some of the most used materials in this style of interior decor, are chrome, brass, aluminum, and stainless steel. These metals are very common in accessories such as lamps, tables, vases, photo frames, or even in certain details of the furniture. The use of exotic woods is also important.

Velvet fabrics that convey glamor are joined with organic and round shapes in pieces of furniture such as daybeds, armchairs, and sofas. Other materials that we could mention in this style are glass, leather, and mirror. Oil paintings, table lamps, bedroom furniture, and finely detailed furniture is quite common in this style.

3.WALLS AND FLOORS

Paying attention to the decoration of floors and walls is a fairly simple way to integrate a style such as Art Deco in your home.

From using wallpaper with geometric shapes or painting the walls with the right colors, as well as floor coverings, are details that will give a very original touch to this style of decoration.

4.FURNITURE AND DECORATIVE ACCESSORIES

Making the right selection of furniture and decorative accessories is very important, since the total success of the decoration of our home will depend on this.

At this point, I must mention that lamps play a leading role in the Art Deco style. The idea is that they have original designs and shapes some type of metal. Gold and silver colors are preferred for lamps in this style, and if they have blown glass shades it is much better.

The lamps will give your décor a beautiful look, as will the integration of mirrors. As for the furniture, my recommendation is that you opt for elegantly designed furniture that combines wood with leather upholstery or delicate fabrics such as velvet. But of course, it must match perfectly with the range of colors you use on the walls.

Décor made from wood, silver, bronze, and iron will help you define the Art Deco style in your home.

I think that Art Deco also has some touches of classic and vintage style. If you want to integrate the Art Deco style into the decoration of your home, keep in mind that the furniture used for this style has defined lines and in terms of materials, we find everything from exotic woods to metals. Materials that are easy to combine with the environment and that maintain the aesthetic values of the Art Deco style.

If you like luxury, this style is for you. I encourage you to give your home the sophisticated aesthetics of the Art Deco style.
