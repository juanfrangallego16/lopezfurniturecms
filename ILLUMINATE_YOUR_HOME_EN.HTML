BASIC TIPS TO ILLUMINATE YOUR HOME

Did you know that good lighting can make your home look more attractive?
The truth is that lighting is not only important to be able to carry out our daily tasks. There is no denying that it is also a must for your home to look amazing.
Giving a little light to your home is not a strange update. Itâ€™s simple if you follow these basic tips to illuminate your home. It is not just about placing a lamp in the middle of a room and thinking that the matter is solved, but about making your home look more attractive taking into account some important points to achieve correct distribution of lighting in all areas of your home.
To illuminate your home you must also take into account the different types of light that exist and choose the most appropriate for each space.
Remember that for each place in the house there is an ideal type of lighting. For example,warm light is ideal in spaces meant for relaxation. In the case of cold and white light, it must be present in spaces where there is usually more activity. For example, the living room, kitchen, bathroom, or office.
Good lighting not only depends on the distribution but on the type of product we use. Today, energy saving is one of the points that are taken into account when choosing between the types of lights that exist on the market. We recommend that, above all, you choose quality products. Many people tend to choose products a little cheaper that do not provide the expected result. Hence why many say "cheap is expensive."
Follow these basic tips to illuminate your home and get ready to see every corner of your home shine.

1.Variety of Lamps

Keep in mind that the lighting in your home can also be enriched through the combination of several models of lamps. Do not stay with just one type of lighting!

Dare to play with the different styles of lamps; whether they are ceiling, wall, or table and with the shades of light they provide you.

It is relevant that you identify the points of light that you want to highlight so that you can obtain a wonderful result.

For example, in the kitchen, the integrated light rails and hanging pendant lights look very good. I recommend you be careful with the shadows. That is why it is good to avoid placing them above the work area.

Good lighting is essential in bath rooms or dressing rooms. I recommend you play with the lighting in the ceiling and around your mirror.

2.Place a Light Dimmerr

The light that illuminates a room can influence our mood. Light dimmers are the optimal level of comfort in your home. With this small device, we can control the brightness of our lighting to accommodate different moods.

A light dimmer is a good investment for your home. There is no doubt that it is perfect to have it in the living room and the bedrooms. With it, you can modulate the intensity of the light according to the time of day and the mood you want to create.

It is an experience to create different moods in the same space of your home.

Remember! It is about your home, your refuge, your space. So you don't have to limit yourself when decorating and using artifacts or tools that will help us improve all the spaces in our home.

3.Natural Lighting is a Must

Let the light flow freely in every corner of your home. Clean windows and remove objects that obstruct its path so that it can illuminate your entire home well.

Natural light brings well-being and warmth to any home, in addition to giving the home a special charm.

But, in addition to the aesthetic advantages, the impact that adequate natural light has on the energy efficiency of the home is also very significant.

One point to keep in mind is that exposure to natural light in a home brings many benefits. A very important benefit is that it kills bacteria that grow in dark and humid environments.

Although it is good, it should not be abused either. Good natural lighting must provide enough daylight to the room; but without causing glare, hot temperatures, or other negative effects.

4.Decorate with Candles

Candles are also an option in which we can add warmth to our home and if they are aromatic they will also provide a pleasant fragrance. Candles are one of the fastest, simplest, and most affordable resources that exist to reinvent the decoration of any corner.

It seems overrated, but with an element as simple as candles you can do real wonders. Due to the wide variety of colors, sizes, and shapes, they can be used in multiple ways in the home.

With some simple cylindrical white candles, we can decorate the entire home or achieve different moods according to the smells.

It is interesting that candles, although they were invented centuries ago, are never overlooked. There are only a few decorations that look good in any corner of our home. Candles look great in any space.

5.Take Care of the Location of the Lamps

It is important that the lamps are not over the heads of your guests. This can be a bit annoying and will create strange shadows. The most frequent doubts about lighting have to do with the right type of light for each environment. As well as the number of lamps, the size, and the recommended distance between them.

When you are going to choose the size of the lamps you have to look at the available space. They cannot be so large that they dominate the entire area, nor too small to get lost in it.

Lighting your home may seem complicated, but if you follow these basic tips to illuminate your home, you will make it look cozy and comfortable.
