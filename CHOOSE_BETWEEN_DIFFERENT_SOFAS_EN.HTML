HOW TO CHOOSE BETWEEN THE DIFFERENT TYPES OF SOFAS WITH CHAISE LOUNGE?

There is no doubt that convenience and comfort are very important, as are style and creativity. A beautiful sofa with a chaise lounge can guarantee all these characteristics.
Interestingly, the term chaise longue comes from French, and literally means "long chair". If you look closely, it is precisely what stands out from this type of sofa.
One of its characteristics is that the sofas with a chaise lounge have an extension in one of their seats, configuring a sectional, which will allow whoever makes use of this seat to stretch their legs as if it were a divan.
It is a piece of furniture that provides numerous advantages. In terms of space, it takes advantage of the corners; it can also offer storage capacity if ordered with a compartment and there are generally a wide variety of designs, sizes, and finishes with many functions.
In this article, I want to explain in detail all the types of sofas with chaise lounge that are on the market and of course some tips so you can select the one that fits perfectly into your home.

1.CHAISE LOUNGE WITH BED

For many it is a perfect complement, especially in homes where an extra bed is needed.

It offers all the comforts and spaciousness of a chaise lounge, but with the functionality of a sofa bed, as part of the seat extends until it fits at the height of the longest part, forming a wide surface you can sleep on.

They are the first choice for practical people who are also looking for comfort and modernity. As sofas, they are the most spacious and comfortable that can be found today.

2.SOFAS WITH REVERSIBLE CHAISE LOUNGE

It is a model that is very functional and versatile, since it offers a movable module, which can be placed both to the left and to the right of the sofa and can even be used individually when you need an extra seat.

If you think about it, it could be a very original way of redistributing the decoration and the space as you please.

It is the perfect option if you are looking for freedom to transform your living room or if you are not sure about the composition you need. You get the most out of your sofa.

As I mentioned before, you can separate it from the sofa and get the most out of it by allowing more people to sit comfortably. Or you can put it to the left or right, reinventing your room over and over again.

3.CHAISE LOUNGE WITH STORAGE

Some chaise lounges offer storage that, precisely, incorporates in the part that protrudes, or even in the arm or on both sides, depending on the model.

Thus, by acquiring a chaise lounge with storage, you are obtaining a functional piece of furniture, which offers you a large storage capacity for all kinds of objects, whether blankets, cushions, or toys.

4.RECLINING CHAISE LOUNGE

I particularly think that you will find the greatest comfort with this type of chaise lounge.

These are models that are equipped with reclining seats such as reclining armchairs, controlled by a motor that allows the footrest to be raised, the backrest, and at times the headrest to be reclined to the desired height with the push of a button or pull of a lever.

This is a piece of furniture that is functional, comfortable, and ergonomic. The comfort of an electric reclining chaise lounge sofa lies in the versatility of postures that you can adopt when sitting or lying down with minimal effort.

5.CHAISE LOUNGE WITH SLIDING SEATS

Chaise lounge sofas equipped with sliding seats are those models in which seats can be removed individually, giving rise to a much larger seating space that allows you to stretch your legs.

Generally, this functionality is manual, having to pull them out. The sliding chaise lounge sofas are functional and comfortable and adapt to all styles and to any home.

6.BASED ON THE USE YOU WANT TO GIVE IT

I recommend you think carefully about choosing the chaise lounge model based on the use you want especially if you and your family like to enjoy your time at home resting in the comfort of the living room. So, the best thing you can do is invest in a quality sofa, with the greatest comfort, such as a reclining chaise lounge or with sliding seats.

If, on the other hand, you are looking for a place to hide an extra bed, for when you receive guests, you should opt for a chaise lounge with a bed.

And of course, it would also be good to pay attention to the number of members who regularly use the space, since this should determine the number of seats on the sofa.

7.TAKE DECORATION INTO ACCOUNTS

Another factor that you should consider is that the chaise lounge you choose matches the decoration of the space in which you want to place it.

For this, you should consider what the layout of the longest part will be, as well as other aspects such as color, textures, and finishes among others.

As I mentioned in previous articles, it is also good to keep all this in mind if you have pets or small children, since the upholstery plays a fundamental role.


One of the most important aspects to choosing a sofa with a chaise lounge is space. Usually, a large piece of furniture requires certain dimensions to fit properly in the space.

There is no doubt that, thanks to the wide variety and types of chaise lounges available on the market, finding the sofa you have always dreamed of will be a simple task and remember that it is valid to seek the help of experts in the field.
