CREATE A FRESH AND WELCOMING ENVIRONMENT IN YOUR HOME THIS SUMMER

From my perspective, interior design is a fun and creative activity, which gives us the opportunity to express our personality and create cozy and pleasant environments in our home. However, sometimes it isn’t easy to adapt our spaces to the different seasons of the year, especially in summer, when the heat and light can be oppressive.

There is no doubt that summer is a season that invites us to enjoy the outdoors, nature, sun, and water fun. For this reason, when we decorate our home in the summer, it is important to create spaces that transmit freshness, light, joy, and relaxation. This way our home becomes a refuge to rest from the heat and stress, but also a place to share moments with our family and friends.

To give our home a summer touch, we can look for inspiration in different styles and trends that adapt to this time of year. Some of these could be the Mediterranean, Nordic, boho, or rustic style. All of these have the use of light and vibrant colors, the use of natural and recycled materials, floral and geometric prints, handmade and vintage pieces, and lots of vegetation.

In this article I will provide you with some ideas and tips for decorating your home in summer, considering aspects such as color, materials, textiles, and accessories. I will also give you some ideas on how to apply these elements in each of the living spaces.

1. COLORS

Unquestionably, color is a fundamental element in interior design, since it is true that it can influence our state of mind and, of course, the thermal sensation of space. When summer arrives, the ideal is to opt for light and fresh colors that provide light and spaciousness in the home. White, beige, grey, blue, and green tones are perfect for creating a pleasant, relaxing, and serene atmosphere. We can also add some touches of more vibrant colors, such as yellow, orange, or fuchsia, to bring joy and dynamism to our home. These colors can be applied both on the walls and on the furniture, and accessories.

2. MATERIALS

Choosing the perfect materials for our furniture and accessories is also important because that is one of the ways to achieve the proper interior design for the summer. My recommendation is that you opt for natural, light materials that transmit freshness and harmony to the environment. Wood, bamboo, rattan, wicker, or cotton are some examples of materials that we can use with our sofas, tables, chairs, shelves, or decorative baskets. These materials also have the advantage of being ecological and sustainable, which contributes to caring for the environment.

3. TEXTILES

It is said that textiles are another key aspect in interior design in summer. Textiles help us dress our home and give it warmth and comfort. My recommendation for summer would be to avoid heavy and dark fabrics, which absorb heat and create a stifling environment. Ideally, select light and breathable fabrics, such as linen, silk, or organic cotton. We can use these fabrics in the curtains, accent pillows, rug, or blankets. For example, we can opt for floral, geometric or ethnic motifs to give our home a more summer touch.

4. THE ACCESSORIES

Accessories are the perfect complement to our home this summer. Accessories allow us to personalize our space and give it fresh air without having to spend a lot of money or make big changes. Some examples of accessories that we can incorporate into our decor are natural or artificial plants, scented candles, vases with fresh or dried flowers, and art with marine or tropical landscapes. These accessories help us create a more welcoming and joyful environment in our home.

5. THE LIVING ROOM

There is no doubt that the living room is the center of our homes, where we spend the most time with visitors. Therefore, it is essential to create a cozy and comfortable environment. A good idea for the summer could be to change the cushion covers and blankets for other lighter and fresher fabrics, such as linen or cotton. White, beige, and blue colors are ideal for transmitting peace. We can also add some more vivid touches of color with accessories such as rugs, lamps, or wall art. Plants are another key element to bring life to our living room. Keep in mind that this season the ideal would be to have plants that withstand heat, such as cacti, succulents, or small palm trees.

6. THE KITCHEN

I believe that the kitchen is one of the places where the heat accumulates when summer arrives, so the ideal would be to find ways to cool it down. One option would be to paint the walls or furniture in a light or pastel color, such as white, mint green, or light pink (for those feeling riskier). Another option is to have ceramic tiles with geometric or floral patterns that will give a fun and original touch to the kitchen. You can also renew textiles such as cloths, tablecloths, or curtains to a more cheerful style. And let's not forget the potted aromatic plants such as rosemary, basil, or thyme that, in addition to a great décor piece, will also serve to cook delicious dishes.

7. THE BEDROOM

It is the most intimate and personal space in our homes, where we seek to rest and relax. For summer, you can select bedding that is breathable and soft to the touch, such as linen or organic cotton. As I mentioned before, light colors are the most appropriate to create a feeling of spaciousness and cleanliness. You can also incorporate some details with brighter colors or summer prints that bring joy. For example, some cushions with floral or geometric motifs, a quilt or blanket with sailor stripes or a fringed plaid. I encourage you to add lighter curtains and let in natural light without losing privacy. Plants are also welcomed in the bedroom as long as they are not too large or require high humidity.

For me, it is important that we give our home a new look and connect it with the season. With a few simple changes in colors, materials, textiles, and accessories we can achieve a fresh, bright, and harmonious decor that makes us feel at peace to be able to enjoy our home. In addition, interior design in the summer is of great importance for our physical and emotional well-being. It is not only about creating beautiful and aesthetic spaces but also functional and comfortable ones that help us beat the heat and enjoy our home more. Remember to be inspired by different styles and trends that adapt to summer according to your tastes and preferences. The important thing is that we manage to create an environment in accordance with the time of year in which we are living without losing our essence or our comfort.
