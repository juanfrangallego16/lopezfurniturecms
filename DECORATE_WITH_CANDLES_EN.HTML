DECORATE WITH CANDLES TO LIGHT UP YOUR HOME

If you are anything like me, you think that candles are perfect as a decorative piece in any corner of the home. Decorating with candles is a total success, and they are perfect for decorating centerpieces, shelves, and more.
Candles are an accessory to decorate with that will look good in the different corners of your home. Even outdoors it looks good because you can either illuminate the edge of the pool, mark paths, light parties, or decorate the terrace. They are one of the accessories that will help you make a difference and will make your home shine.
Do you think your home is missing something, and you don't know what it is? Feel like it needs a touch of warmth? A good solution for this would be to illuminate the spaces of your home and your special moments with the wide variety of candles that exist.
Adding a few candles in your interior decoration can make a big impact as they can transform and bringing life to any environment. You can use them anywhere and you can be sure that they will never go unnoticed.
By using candles, you will see how they will create a feeling of warmth in your home. They are ideal to give life to even the most forgotten corner in the home. They are perfect to create a romantic atmosphere.
Candles are never forgotten even though they were invented centuries ago. Although they were created to illuminate in times when electricity did not yet exist, today they can bring style, warmth, and beauty to any space of your home. You can make them your great allies to create much more relaxed environments.
If you want something that adds a feminine touch to your home decor, candles and flowers will always be your best choice. There are those who use them to decorate small spaces in the bathroom by placing a couple of candles next to the perfumes on a tray. This keeps it well organized and if you still add a small arrangement of natural flowers, it is sure to create a super pleasant environment.
If you decorate the tables with elements such as vases or pots, you can add some very simple candles. You can even place them in glass containers to give a touch of elegance to the decoration.
Brighten up your best moments!

1.SIZES AND COLORS

There is a wide variety of candles: elongated, square, round, cylindrical or star-shaped, floating, wax, aromatic, white, or colored candles. We could spend a whole day listing the variety of candles that exist today. If your idea is to integrate them with the rest of the decoration, it is necessary that before buying them you define the place or places where you want to locate them so you can make a successful selection.

You can even play with the color palette of textiles, books, even flowers, or vases in the room where you want to place them. We are sure that you will love the result you will get.

Candles are decorative and versatile items. By changing the color of them, you will be able to bring contrasts and tones to your rooms according to the decoration or the season.

2.AROMAS AND EMOTIONS

In the market you will not only find ordinary candles, but you will also find a wide variety of scented and aromatic candles. These types of candles are indisputably capable of generating emotions and stimulating the senses, such as smell.

In addition, they will help you create a pleasant environment, filling the corners with fragrances and perfumes. There is a wide variety of aromas among which you can select the essence of your preference. You can even find them with the aroma of sea breeze, freshly cut grass, coffee, among others.

Something that is worth noting is that citrus aromas are ideal for hot seasons, as they help to refresh the environments.

The variety of motifs to decorate with candles is practically endless. Lighting is one of the keys to decorating and, if done in the right way, it serves to generate emotions. You can take advantage of the candlelight to create intimate and delicate environments. They can help you feel more relaxed, more at home.

Some scents can convey emotions and they can even bring back memories. Those that contain scent with delicious floral notes are perfect for the living room and citrus scents are perfect for the bathroom to give a sense of cleanliness.

3.WHERE CAN YOU PLACE THEM?

You can place them in any corner of your home. They look beautiful in the living room, on the coffee table, on a side table, on shelves in the bedrooms, in the bathroom, on the terrace, reading spaces, or a home office. If used properly, you will achieve a wonderful result.

When decorating you can choose candles or materials that are already decorated. You can use candle holders or simpler candles and create beautiful wrappings using the most diverse materials. Just let your imagination flow.

You can personalize the candles and create lanterns out of paper bags. This is a great idea to decorate for parties or special moments.

There are those who use upside down glass cups to decorate the dining room table. You can even opt for beautiful floating candles that you can place in beautiful glass containers, and you will see how they add a touch of elegance to your d√©cor.

It never fails when you place candles in glass lanterns with gold frames. This idea always offers a touch of sophistication.
