IDEAS FOR YOUR DINING ROOM YOU WILL LOVE

Today I want to give a little inspiration to those who remain undecided and still don't know what style to give to this important space in the home.

Since I was very little I have always considered the dining room as one of the most endearing spaces in the home. I must say that this thought arose thanks to the influence of my paternal grandmother, of whom I keep the most beautiful memories and most of them developed in the dining room.

I remember having delicious meals in the company of family at a nice dining table which still exists to this day. I keep these memories in my heart. Yes, as I said before, the table is still there, but from the people who shared it; some are gone, and others take different paths. But those memories remain, and we will never forget the wonderful memories we created together.

Choosing the ideal dining table is very important, as it will help you create special memories that will accompany you throughout your lifeâ€™s pleasant moments with family and friends that will make you smile when you remember.

To choose the decoration and the style that we will give to the dining room we must consider all the furniture; however, I think it is interesting to give it a personal touch.

In this article, I will share with you some ideas for the dining room that you will love. All with different styles, but if you look carefully, you will notice that they have a common denominator: a lot of style for your home that will not disappoint you.

1. DO YOU HAVE AN OPEN DINING ROOM?

I am one who when we have a large space, the task of selecting furniture for the dining room is less complicated. Basically, we are less likely to make mistakes in the decoration. A good idea would be to choose a beautiful dining table and if it is extendable even better. This way you can enjoy it with the family and in special moments in which some friends will join. And why not? Opting for flashy designs would also be a good idea. If your purpose is to achieve a neutral environment that you never get tired of, there is no doubt that wood and the color black are two safe bets.

2. WOULD YOU LIKE A RETRO FEEL?

The retro design is based on pieces inspired by the interior decoration of the 50s, 60s, and 70s, when this style became relevant. Unquestionably it was a time of innovation in which furniture had sinuous and original shapes, color took center stage, and a revolution in materials and textures began. This created unique and enveloping textures and they do not have to be pieces manufactured at that time, but rather they can be designs that recall this movement without neglecting new influences.

From my point of view, this is a style that should be integrated into the decoration of spaces in the home through small doses. Lamps, sculptures, some vibrant color, a beautiful dining table that perfectly represents this style, a correct selection of chairs, and beautiful wooden furniture are pieces that provide these small touches that drastically change your home decor.

3. DO YOU LOVE STRAIGHT LINES?

This point is perfect for modern dining rooms, because in this style there is nothing better than the combination of a classic design. Undoubtedly, the effect of a dining room with a straight-line design will make you fall in love as soon as you see it.

It is easier to find the symmetry of the environments with straight-lined furniture. I am sure that a square or rectangular dining table is very flexible when it comes to combining it with other decorative elements or with the shape of the room itself.

4. A BEAUTIFUL NORDIC DINING ROOM

Did you know that on a base of white furniture with touches of wood you can create a perfect Nordic dining room? I have no doubt that the simplicity of the Nordic style is capable of conquering almost all of us. Just imagine furniture with a clean design, on which you can mess around with some shade of gray that will add character to the decoration of your dining room.

If there is something special that defines Nordic-style environments, it is undoubtedly naturalness and the desire to increase natural lighting. These two characteristics are the starting point to create a perfect Nordic environment.

If you want your dining room to become a clear example of the Nordic style from a decorative point of view, it is very important that you choose a range of soft and light colors. In addition to white, go for a gray tone or some pastel color, especially for the walls. Another point that you should consider is to stay away from darker colors. Doing this will get your dining room to inherit a perfect feeling of spaciousness.

5. DIFFERENT CHAIRS

A special trick to give the dining room more dynamism is to mix different models of chairs around the table. You can opt for painted wooden models mixed with unpainted ones. Or you can also combine chairs of different colors, shapes, and designs.

One key worth looking at is to try to get the chairs to have similar dimensions, even if they differ in design. In this way we establish a visual order that is pleasant.

If you think about it, you will see that our lifestyle and personal tastes play an important role when choosing a dining room for your home. It is necessary to be very clear that straight lines and curves do not reflect the same thing.

To achieve an acceptable decoration and to fulfill everyone's taste, it is important to be clear about the ideas you want to achieve. the materials, shapes, and style must be to your liking. It is uncomfortable to live in an environment that does not represent us or that is out of context with respect to the style of the rest of the home.

When decorating the dining room, the idea is to have a clear plan. It is not necessary to complicate our lives. My recommendation is that you simply choose those items that are useful, functional, and aesthetically appealing. After all, there are dining rooms of all styles and for all tastes.
