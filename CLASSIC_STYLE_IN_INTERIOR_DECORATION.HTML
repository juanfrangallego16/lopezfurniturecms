CLASSIC STYLE IN INTERIOR DECORATION

The classic style does not go out of style because it has maintained its essence for centuries. Its indisputable timeless elegance is totally outside of fashions, it can be adapted to the personality of those who will inhabit the house. This style is based on noble materials, in which Baroque and Rococo furniture stand out, luxurious floors, ornate walls, and a variety of accessories; but always with a balance that gives visual strength to the spaces.
The classic style has many decorative possibilities and is capable of creating spaces full of luxury and sophistication. In addition, this style cannot be without wood, a fundamental decorative element that can be present in furniture, walls, floors, and accessories. You can be sure that wood will bring nobility and warmth to any space. If you are attracted to sophistication and antiques, this is the style for you. The classic-style interiors attract with their colors and imperial distinction. This style can be quite expensive, which is why it is usually adopted by people who enjoy a good economic status, who have certainly established tastes.
To illuminate a classic-style interior design dare to use crystal chandeliers and transparent stone lamps. Classic style chandeliers are true works of art. In addition to the ceiling lamps, in these spaces, you will also see bronze chandeliers and torches with silk shades. Achieving a classic style in your home can be a simple task if you dare to follow a series of tips and respect certain characteristics in the decoration. Here we bring you some points that you should keep in mind when decorating with this style.

1.MATERIALS

In this style, wood is the main protagonist, as is silk, porcelain, velvet, textiles, and other materials that can provide a distinguished and elegant touch to the decoration. Do not forget that you can also make use of metallic elements in gold and silver since they provide that same elegance and distinction.

Unquestionably, in this style, light colors and furniture with rounded and sinuous shapes prevail, as well as chromatic contrasts and mixes of textures and fabrics.

You cannot miss the floors and details in wood, moldings on walls and ceilings, and the borders to separate the wall into two parts or baseboards.

Many textile elements such as cotton, linen, and silk are also used as accessories with very elegant finishes.

2.FURNITURE

Some elements that cannot be absent in this style like wooden furniture in the dining room, a large mirror, chandeliers, ceiling lamps, art, and antiques.

The pieces of the classic style are the consoles, siphons, wing chairs, trunks, armchairs, headboards, bookcases, chests, dressers, headboards, and large wooden tables with turned legs. Large beds with wrought iron or wooden headboards are part of creating your bedroom. Other key elements are large mirrors, armchairs, blinds, lamps with lampshades, poufs, stools, & paintings.

The traditional chandelier or chandelier is another essential piece in the classic style. Also chandeliers, large mirrors, antique pieces, art, and other accessories with classic, timeless, and antique style.

3.RECHARGED SPACES

The interior decoration with classic style is the opposite of the minimalist style. In this style details, furniture, decoration, and accessories abound.

Remember that the space that we generate must be powerful and with a lot of pomposities. So in general they are usually accompanied by borders to decorate interiors and decorative wall furniture.

In this style, everything must be perfectly balanced, so you will need spacious and well-lit rooms to generate a harmonious atmosphere.

4.COLORS

The classic style is characterized by colors of light tones: cream, beige, green, gray, or even light yellow. The softness of the tones is relevant to preserve the atmosphere of harmony that you want to reign in your rooms.Its combination with wooden furniture, with a color similar to those indicated, is always very successful. In small spaces and with little light, a classic style with light colors is perhaps the best option to obtain space and luminosity.

Another trend can be the darker colors, with dark wood furniture, velvet textiles, masculine and sober touches, chromatic contrasts, and damask prints.

5.LIGHTING

For this style, the best option is warm light, which maintains the atmosphere provided by the materials, furniture, and decoration. You can use low-intensity spotlights and lamps with shades that can achieve a more welcoming light.

Lighting in the classic style is characterized by the use of highly elaborate luminaires with shiny materials. Something you may notice is that this style abounds in crystals and metals such as bronze or chrome.

Keep in mind that one of the characteristics of this style is symmetry. That is why you will have to choose furniture sets, accessories, and also light fixtures. It is the typical case of the use of wall sconces.

It is a style that has decorative elements that do not leave anyone indifferent. Their designs can transport us to other times and other places and make our dream of their daring aesthetics that pay tribute to aristocratic, elegant, modern, and always current styles.
