HOW TO CHOOSE THE PERFECT ARMCHAIR FOR YOUR READING CORNER?

Do you like reading and would love to be able to do it comfortably in a good armchair? As a fan of reading, I have the pleasure of talking to you about which are the best armchairs for reading.
Before fully addressing this main topic about the best armchairs for reading, it is important to know how to keep good posture during your Reading time. In other words, if we want to take care of our posture, it is also important to maintain an adequate position while reading.
That said, I want to remind you that it is not recommended to read lying down, since both face down and face up we expose different parts of our body to unnecessary pressure, such as the neck, among others. On the contrary, reading sitting down is advisable, and you can have full confidence that comfort is guaranteed with a good chair.
To be a bit more specific, it is recommended to read sitting with your back and head straight. Also, it is super important to make sure you are in a place with good lighting, which allows you to read without straining your eyes.
Why is an armchair recommended for reading? Because these adapt to the body and envelop it, offering you the greatest comfort, but maintaining a good posture of course.
In this article I am going to talk to you about the characteristics that a good armchair should have so that it can occupy the reading space in your home.
I also want to take the opportunity to give you some recommendations so that you can create your own reading corner at home.

What would be the characteristics of a good chair?

1.COMFORT

There is no doubt that comfort is one of the key factors in determining which are the best armchairs for reading. Comfort can be achieved thanks to the right materials, and it is necessary to pay attention to the ergonomics of the sofa and its functionalities.

Relax armchairs are a perfect option. They are specifically designed for a personalized degree of comfort, upholstered in fabrics or leather, and offer various systems of optimal ergonomics, such as: elevation, manual or electric recline (to keep the feet up).

In addition, it must be taken into account that within the category of relax armchairs there are multiple options including, in addition to footrest and reclining backrest, functions such as: reclining up and forward function, lumbar heating function, adjustable headrest, among others.

2.EASY TO CLEAN

For many, part of the comfort comes from not having to pay much attention to chair care. My recommendation is that if you want an easy-to-clean armchair, you should opt for fabrics with technology that offers advanced protection to the upholstery, covering each fiber of the fabric with an invisible molecular layer, to prevent dirt from penetrating making it easy to remove. This kinds of fabric should allow you to clean with a cloth and a little water. Leather is also a great alternative that is easy and comfortable to maintain.

3.AESTHETICS

In addition to comfort, aesthetics is becoming increasingly important, and although we are talking about the best armchairs for reading, getting an armchair that matches the environment.

A beautiful design can go hand in hand with comfort. In fact, there are reclining armchairs that take aesthetics into account, but still provide the perfect ergonomics and the greatest comfort for moments of reading.

4.RESISTANT STRUCTURE

This point has to do with the quality of the product. You must take into account the passage of time and that if you want a durable chair you must invest in quality. Just make sure that the type of material it is made of is resistant and durable over time.

One of the best chairs is made with a solid wood frame, since it offers great robustness, preventing it from deforming with use and the passage of time. Another option would be aluminum or iron structures, which offer great resistance.

5.BREADTH

An armchair that allows us to feel more comfortable and with freedom of movement, this is definitely another of the characteristics that the best armchairs for reading should have.

It is indisputable that a large part of the relax armchairs fulfill this function perfectly,which is not only observed in their wide seat, but also in the size of the seat.

Recommendations to create your own reading corner at home.

1.GOOD LIGHTING

There is no doubt it is very important to locate your reading corner in a place with good natural light. This is the best option for Reading. In this way you will prevent your eyes from getting tired, and I am sure that you will be able to concentrate better.

You can also add enough artificial light to the space you choose. Currently, there are lighting products that offer you the possibility of regulating the amount of light, as well as the tone. A more than adequate option if you want to achieve different environments.

For this particular space, it is best to choose between two options: a floor lamp or a table lamp.

2.BOOKCASE OR SHELVES

If you like to read, your reading corner cannot be without a bookcase or shelf, in which you can place all your books, and you can access them comfortably whenever you want. This piece of furniture, in addition to being functional, will provide style and character to the space.

There is a wide variety of shelves on the market, with different sizes and styles, with wood finishes, metal structures. You just have to select the one that best suits the style of your home.

3.SIDE TABLE

Once you have what you consider to be the best chair to read based on your tastes and needs, you should consider purchasing a side table.

It is definitely an essential accessory, where you can place everything you need for your reading.

A side table will be your help if you want to eat a snack while you read. If you are savoring a delicious coffee or tea, you will surely need to have a place to put it and a small table will help you.

4.DECORATION STYLE

Having mentioned all the basic elements for a reading corner, it really doesn't take much to be able to spend hours and hours enjoying a book. If you want to personalize this space and offer it character and style, it's time to add some decorative touches.

An excellent idea is to place a rug under the armchair, add a matching cushion, a blanket, a plant, and pictures that combine with the theme, among other ideas.
