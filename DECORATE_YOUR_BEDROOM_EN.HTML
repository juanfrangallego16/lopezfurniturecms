5 STYLES TO DECORATE YOUR BEDROOM

Decorating the bedroom is not only about creating a beautiful and pleasant space to the eye. In my opinion, it’s about having a room that gives you good rest and becomes that haven of peace that we all need in the end of the day.
The process of selecting a style to decorate a room depends a lot on the needs and tastes of each person. As they say, "between tastes and colors the authors have not written." I like that my bedroom is dominated by white colors, but some of my esteemed readers may find warm tones more pleasant.
The fact is that there is no one style for your bedroom by obligation, on the contrary, there are many styles that you can apply, and you can merge to create the perfect bedroom for you.
Regardless of the style you select, a good bed and a quality mattress is essential in any bedroom. At this point, take your time to make the best decision.
On many occasions when it comes time to decorate, we can get lost among so many ideas or even in the absence of them. But we must not despair if this happens to us. Remember to breathe deeply and if possible, you can seek help from a professional in the field or perhaps you will find some answers in my article.
One piece of advice I can give you is that before decorating your bedroom, ask yourself, “will at the end of the day I find that refuge in my bedroom”, the place where you will find yourself, where you can relax, even distract yourself from everyday life. You will see how little by little the ideas will flow and you will realize that it is what you really need in your room.
Today I will show you a brief review of 5 styles to decorate your bedroom, among which perhaps you can find the one that best suits your personality and your needs. I hope these ideas inspire you.

1.MINIMALIST

In this style less is more since you will only need furniture and essential items. My recommendation is that you decide on straight and simple lines, use a neutral color palette, and do not forget that good lighting is also important.

In this style, forget about the ostentatious, keep in mind that everything that you integrate into this type of decoration must have a clear function. The minimalist style is perfect to relax and by selecting the right bed and accessories you can create an elegant and very pleasant space.

2.VINTAGE

The passion for the old and the original is always a success when it comes to beautifying any space in your home and a vintage bedroom will not be the exception to the rule.

The decoration with vintage style will have certain variations according to your personality, unlike the previous style in vintage there are many elements that you can apply to create the ideal space.

The key to vintage bedrooms is to avoid furniture with modern lines. Furniture with a worn, robust look and elegant and delicate curves, with floral carvings, are essential in a vintage bedroom.

3.MODERN

If there is a space in your home that should really reflect your taste, your personal style and gives you the greatest comfort, that is your bedroom. One point that you should keep in mind is that this style is in constant renovation. In this case I recommend using furniture with straight lines and when it comes to lighting you could opt for recessed spotlights that will go according to the style of the decor.

The absence or presence of a headboard can completely change the style of a bedroom, as it is a detail that does not go unnoticed. Modernity demands to be practical, leaving aside other decorative styles that are too ostentatious and overloaded.

One of the easiest ways to give your room a modern decor is to opt for textured, colorful bedding in attractive fabrics.

The colors can be based on your personal taste, but neutral tones dominate, such as white, beige, or gray. You can also apply some touches of color in accessories or on a single wall.

4.RUSTIC

The rustic style is one of the most welcoming, warm, calm, and comfortable in decoration. In the rustic style, wood cannot be absent, decorating the bedroom with this style is an excellent idea since it will provide you good rest and it is also perfect for this fall.

In the case of textiles, I recommend opting for natural fabrics such as cotton to help you create a warm and harmonious atmosphere in the bedroom. It’s very important that in this style the relationship with nature with the views through the windows is obvious. The use of plants and natural fabrics is also ideal.

The best thing about rustic decoration is that it will bring a lot of warmth and comfort to your bedroom. In fact, it does not matter if you live in the country or in the city, you can always opt for this style of decoration to create a cozy atmosphere where you can take refuge every day.

5.SCANDINAVIAN

The light and neutral color palette is the ideal basis for Scandinavian bedroom design. If white seems too boring for you, gray is a great alternative. It is perfect for both walls and floors, furniture, and decor in general.

In a bedroom with this style, neutral bedding cannot be absent and some decorative details such as paintings, rugs, or cushions are the ones that give some contrast to the environment.

Simplicity, light colors, and the use of natural wood furniture are the key to success in the Scandinavian style.

Regardless of the style you choose to decorate your bedroom, the important thing is that in the end you get the space you want so much and that you give yourself nights with good rest and sweet dreams.
