GREAT IDEAS FOR SMALL SPACES

Are you one of those who think that to live in a small home requires you to give up comfort? Then these great ideas for small spaces are for you.

There are a variety of tricks that you can put into practice to make your small spaces look wonderful and in the same way they are super comfortable and with the style that best defines you.

We are 100% sure that these decorations for small spaces will be of great help to make the most of the square footage in your living room, kitchen, bedroom, or bathroom. Apply them and you will see how you will achieve interesting effects in your spaces that at first glance are small.

Each room in the house has its peculiarities, but the key points are in the use of light colors (especially cold or neutral ones) that, as we have mentioned in previous articles, generate the feeling of larger, cleaner, and brighter environments. Of course, another point that you should not miss is the choice of multifunctional furniture (tables, desk or sofa beds) that responds to different needs.

With these great ideas and a little common sense, you will see how your little home will look wonderful and you will be able to enjoy the comfort you deserve to the fullest.

1.Small Living Rooms

A very important point that you must take into account when decorating small spaces is not to overload the environment with unnecessary furniture. You must be clear about the functionality of the furniture and decorations.

Opting for comfortable armchairs instead of sofas, to watch television is something viable and by making the correct selection of these, you can give it personality

If you dare to, use decorative vinyl to break the monotony of a wall.

Remember that you should avoid placing furniture in walking space so that it does not block the flow. Another important trick is to create ceiling space by directing lighting to the ceiling and walls.

Do not forget that it is of the utmost importance that you keep the space orderly and clean. In this way, your spaces create harmony and comfort.

Keep in mind that side tables and folding chairs are ideal for storing and saving space when not in use, so they are a good option to consider for a small home.

A good idea is if instead of large furniture, use shelves and bookcases to store objects.

And do not forget that mirrors help to widen the space, so add them to the decoration of your spaces.

2.Small Kitchens

In small kitchens, you should opt for white artificial lighting of good intensity.

Be careful with kitchen utensils accumulated insight as they create the feeling of disorder and reduced space. So it is good to emphasize the importance of order and cleanliness. It is better to have a piece of furniture or shelf to store them in or you can also use hangers for pans and other kitchen accessories.

You have to avoid placing appliances in passageways. In addition to that, take into account that stainless steel appliances give a greater sense of spaciousness.

And a point that would be good to take into account is to use fancier folding tables and chairs that allow to free up space.

3.Small Bedrooms

Remember to decorate with furniture and objects in light tones, such as beech or pine wood, metal or glass to create the feeling of a spacious room. For this effect, you can elevate your furniture.

If you like curtains and want to add them to the decoration, we recommend using the same color as the walls.

Make the most of the natural light in the room and it would be helpful if the curtain fabric allows outside lighting.

Mirrors are great to gain space in the room. So go ahead and make the best selection of mirrors according to the style you have selected to decorate your spaces. You will love the result.

The decorative vinyl also works well for the room. They can give a point of originality and creativity to the room, without taking away living space.

4.Small Baths

Did you know that forgotten corners are ideal for placing accessories that allow you to store and organize the many items in a bathroom?

If there is no space to locate a built-in cabinet for towels, bathrobes, hairdryer, and cosmetic products, you can opt for less bulky solutions; such as base cabinets or drawers with wheels under the sink. They are a perfect option for a small bathroom.

I encourage you to place small glass, plastic, or wood shelves to have all those frequently used accessories at hand.

One point that you should consider is suitable lighting. The lights that least occupy space are recessed spotlights in the ceiling. They can be combined with a halogen light pointing at a mirror.

The most important thing in creating the perfect spaces for ourselves and our family is to have fun and do it with love and with the best disposition.
