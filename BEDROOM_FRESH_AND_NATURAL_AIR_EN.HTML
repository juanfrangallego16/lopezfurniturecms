DO YOU WANT TO GIVE YOUR BEDROOM A FRESH AND NATURAL AIR?

The arrival of spring is the perfect excuse to give a twist to your bedroom decor , adding that natural and fresh touch of the season. In this wonderful article I will tell you how to give it that change starting from small details and providing decorative solutions with which you will get that beautiful and pleasant spring room.
With the arrival of each season of the year we make changes to the clothes in our closet. We apply changes to the decoration of the bedroom and any other space in the home.
We like to open the windows wide when spring arrives and let the freshness of the morning and the rays of the sun flood every space of our home.
There is no doubt that to increase that feeling of comfort and life that spring gives us, the right textiles are your best allies.
There is no turning back! spring is here and that is why today I bring you some ideas to give your bedroom a spring touch.

1.CHANGE THE BEDDING

Definitely no change is as easy and fast as this one. The time has come to put away your winter bedding, the duvet cover, and other blankets. Replace them with quilts that favor more appropriate ventilation for spring temperatures.

This way you will get a better rest. It is not a secret that excess heat prevents you from sleeping properly. So my recommendation is that you select fabrics made mainly of cotton and linen, the most recommended options for both spring and summer. You will see how its lightness and softness will be extremely comfortable.

2.OPT FOR DARING PRINTS

Take advantage of spring to give more life to your bedroom with prints that leave winter sobriety behind. A good option would be to opt for floral motifs, geometric figures, or ethnic prints and embroidery.

You only need to apply them to decorative cushions, sheets, or some plaid to make your bedroom look extra cheerful. It is much better if they can also be in the colors of the season: pink, turquoise, blue, green, and yellow.

3.FRESH AND VENTILATED BEDROOMS

As the weeks go by and the heat increases, keep the windows closed during the day to prevent your room from getting too hot.

Take advantage of the first hours of the day and the last hours of the afternoon to ventilate and generate air circulation that refreshes your bedroom, thus achieving a healthier environment.

The most appropriate time to ventilate and oxygenate your bedroom ranges between 10 minutes to an hour, depending on its size and the number of people who occupy it. If you can only ventilate for 10 minutes, at least open the windows or balconies.

4.CHOOSE COLD AND BRIGHT COLORS

Preferably use cool colors from the color wheel, such as blues and greens. This way you will be able to visually refresh the bedroom. Blue is the coldest of all, it is perfect for providing a sense of calm, brightness, and relaxation, making it very suitable for bedrooms.

Green is a calm, balanced, and very fresh color thanks to the combination of the properties of the two colors that compose it. In any case, you can mix both proposals with any other shade to generate a wider range of sensations.

5.THE WALLS CAN ALSO BE DRESSED IN SPRING

One of the best options to bring a radical change to your bedroom is to change the color of the walls or put up a wallpaper with fresh and spring motifs.

If you prefer not to take excessive risks, choose neutral, versatile, and timeless tones for the walls or introduce splashes of color in accessories and textures, an option that is also perfect for the rest of the seasons. However, the use of wallpaper will help you reinforce the new perception of your bedroom without having to invest too much, avoiding annoying extra work.

6.TRY TO GIVE IT AN ECO-FRIENDLY STYLE

The use of natural materials, organic pieces, and carefully recycled elements will transform your bedroom into an evocative space, in contact with nature.

In addition, if you choose furniture and accessories of sustainable and ecological origin, you will achieve a healthy environment that will favor a pleasant rest thanks to the absence of polluting materials such as mercury, chrome, or asbestos, elements that seriously harm health.

7.ADD A PERSONAL TOUCH THROUGH SCENTS

This is an original and captivating way to add a personal stamp to your bedroom, which will also provide you with very pleasant sensations of well-being.

Immerse yourself fully in spring by introducing aromas and perfumes in the form of candles, mikado sticks, home fragrances, and even specific perfumes for bedding. The result will surprise you.

If you want to enhance the feeling of relaxation in your bedroom, opt for lavender, rose, and lemon balm perfumes. They are very subtle and fresh aromas that invite you to rest.


The colors, flowers, and aromas of this season definitely lift anyones spirits.

Don't think about it so much and get to work. Dress your bedroom with natural motifs, cheerful colors and even captivating aromas so that it is perfect in the most optimistic season of the year.

I hope this article will motivate you to launch yourself into decorating your bedroom this spring. Thanks for reading and goodbye until the next article.
