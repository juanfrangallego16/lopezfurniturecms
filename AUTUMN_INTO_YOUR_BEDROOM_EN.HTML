BRING AUTUMN INTO YOUR BEDROOM

To me, the bedroom represents my refuge. It can be the space in which I feel safest, my resting place and even represents the place where my dreams are born. For all these reasons, this space deserves special attention when it comes to remodeling. At the end of the day, the changes, or improvements that I make in my bedroom will be like a self-gift to pamper me.
For this part of the home, the furniture that will decorate each corner is very important. It deserves our full attention because it will become the central focus of the bedroom, as you can imagine I’m referring to the bed. Just because it's the place for sweet dreams, it doesn't mean it should be lacking in style.
For those of us who love autumn, it is so much fun to decorate or remodel the bedroom. I recommend that when selecting the furniture for your bedroom keep in mind that there is furniture that will last you for a long time. For that reason, it is important to look for quality.
If you like furniture that doesn’t go out of style and basic colors, let me tell you that this is a good option since this furniture can be combined with décor and details that give your bedroom the magic touch of each season.
All seasons of the year have a charm about them that can be expressed through colors and decorations to change the look of the room. Today I want to give you some information, so you bring autumn into your bedroom.
There is no doubt autumn is the perfect excuse to remodel our bedroom, so instead of thinking about it, it is better to get down to work and start remodeling.

1.AUTUMN COLORS ON THE BED

If you still do not have the bed that will accompany you for years, I recommend you look for the best option you can find. It is essential to find a bed of excellent quality. You can seek guidance on this matter so you can make the best selection.

If you want a unique style, you can go for a complete bedroom set, with the same finishes, the same material, and with the same tonality. Now let us focus on the details that will help you define your style, and what will truly bring that autumn touch to your bedroom.

If you prefer only the bed to combine it with other furniture, my recommendation is that you focus on the headboard of the bed, since this will add personality and character to the bedroom. If you like the vintage style, focus on beautiful upholstery. If you are a minimalist, you can opt for sober and rustic wood.

Now the time has come to put away the fresh covers and sheets that you used during the summer and start taking out the halftime, cotton, or even jersey-textured ones, which are soft and warmer than linen.

If you think about it, bedding has a transforming power in the image of a bedroom. This is a key point to give the room an autumn touch. A good idea would be to add a light quilt in a shade warmer than usual.

You can trust in bedding with shades of brown, orange, reddish, mustard, pink or even with golden sparkles. Also consider the cushions and pillows that you have or will add to your room. Add plaids, blankets, and quilts to your décor. As a recommendation for when the temperature begins to drop, a wool blanket would be of great.

2.SEEK INSPIRATION IN NATURE

Close your eyes for a moment and begin to imagine as if you are looking out the window. Visualize how the trees and leaves leave their green color to embrace the yellow, orange, and brown tones making a beautiful transition from summer to fall.

If there is one undeniably beautiful aspect of the fall, it is the colors it creates. It's hard not to give into its charm, so make the most of the magic of autumn to make your bedroom a warmer and more welcoming place.

Who said flowers are only for spring? In autumn we can also enjoy the flower décor on a table. To make your bedroom beautiful, it is a matter of changing the color palette and choosing flowers with backgrounds in earthy or reddish tones, with details in dark colors such as chocolate, dark green, navy blue, among others.

You can also place an indoor plant in your bedroom and take advantage of the benefits that it brings to your stay, not only because they connect you with nature, but also because it transmits serenity.

The rugs are also a plus when giving style to the bedroom, although the fiber ones are timeless for when autumn arrives, we lean more towards the comfort and well-being offered by a warmer fabric such as wool or even cotton. With the right carpet, the bedroom will be much more comfortable.

Unquestionably, wood plays an important role in this. So, this fall make sure that wood is not absent in your bedroom.

3.ADD CANDLES TO THE DECOR

I think that in most of the articles we have written it has been clear that candles are great for atmosphere. Although they are not for a specific season, it is during the coldest months of the year when the tinkling of its multicolored flame comforts us in a very pleasant way. In addition, candles are the queens when creating more intimate atmospheres, so they are ideal in the bedroom.

You can opt for candles with scents that remind you of autumn, such as eucalyptus, cinnamon, red fruits, figs, and wood. Believe me that this would be like placing the cherry on the cake and the transformation will be complete.

Keep in mind that accessories are necessary for any style of decoration. To bring autumn to the bedroom, I would recommend arranging by mixing dried flowers, candles, and fruits of the season.

At the end of it all, the importance lies in your coziness, a pleasant bedroom that provides you comfort, and harmony.
