IDEAS TO DECORATE YOUR HOME FOR THANKSGIVING

November has arrived! It amazes me how the days go by so fast. The days go by, and we realize that Thanksgiving is just around the corner. It is a very special day, ideal to celebrate with the family, and it is also an excellent day to reflect.
Thanksgiving is one of the most anticipated holidays. On this day families take time to decorate their home to create the perfect atmosphere for this celebration. A day to be grateful for all the good that the year has brought and for what is coming. We also give thanks for the negative events of the year that taught us a lesson.
This year we have many reasons to give thanks. I know that many like me lost a loved one due to the pandemic, but it is also true that many of us have overcome and today we are in good health. Although, those who didn’t survive the fight are not with us we shall still give thanks because they were a part of our life. This year we are prepared to give thanks with our family and closest friends.
We could say that Thanksgiving dinner is the most important of the year and I am sure that many families start preparing several weeks in advance. Perhaps you have already decided on the dinner menu, the drinks, but perhaps you need to prepare for the decorations.
If you are the chef in your home this year, get in tune with this celebration. It is nearly impossible for you to fail if you add autumn into your decor. Due to it being an American celebration, we are in the middle of autumn and the appropriate colors are orange, gold, brown tones, among others.
If you don’t know what to do with the decor for this day let me tell you that this article can be very useful for you. I will provide you with some ideas that you can apply in decorating your home for Thanksgiving.

1.DECORATE WITH AUTUMN COLORS

There is no doubt that colors play a great role in defining the decoration and setting the home for Thanksgiving. My recommendation is that you opt for autumn colors such as browns, oranges, yellows, reds, and/or greens. You will see that by adding the appropriate colors for the season and celebration, the atmosphere of your home changes and begins to get into the spirit of thanksgiving.

These colors can be applied in the different accessories you use in your décor. For example, you can decorate with pumpkins of different sizes and arrangements with dried leaves since they are very typical of this celebration. These colors can be applied into your decor. You can also add them to curtains, cushions, and textiles that are a part of the different spaces in your home.

2.ADD CANDLES

I am very sure that candles are another complement that never fails in any type of decoration. For this holiday you can put together beautiful arrangements accompanying the candles like pumpkins and pineapples. Always try to do it within the autumn hue and creating something cute and original. But I can assure, that not adding candles to your decor will show.

You can even decorate an arrangement with pumpkins, flowers in autumn tones or dried flowers and some candles. I can assure you that with a little creativity you will create some beautiful arrangements.

3.NATURAL OR DRY FLOWERS

Yes, flowers are the decoration that you cannot miss for Thanksgiving. From dried flowers to natural flowers, they are a characteristic of autumn. You could even dare to mix some dry branches with natural flowers, like sunflowers.

I encourage you to add some nuts and some fruit such as apples to your table decor. In the kitchen and bathroom decor you can also place autumn details that will be ideal for your home to be perfectly set for this beautiful celebration.

4.TABLE DECOR

Food is the protagonist of this holiday, and all the family members gather around the table. Therefore, having a beautiful dining room set and decorating it with beautiful centerpieces plays an important role in this holiday. So, look for inspiration if you need it and create a beautiful centerpiece by applying the advice that I am giving you in this article. As a reminder, do not miss the candles, pumpkins, flowers, nuts, apples, and other seasonal fruits too. The important thing is that in the end the result is a warm and welcoming environment.

Remember that this is the table where the family and friends will gather to give thanks before enjoying the traditional dinner.

5.FOR THE EXTERIOR

Go ahead and decorate the entrance to your home. It will turn out to be a huge win when wanting to create the ideal atmosphere for this wonderful celebration. The truth is that in addition to giving it an original touch it can also be fun and create the feeling of a warm and pleasant home.

Many natural elements are used in autumn decorations that are easy to find. So, it is time to get down to work because the days are passing by very quickly and when we least think about it, Thanksgiving arrives.

It is time to start decorating for this special dinner. Consider the ideas that I have shared with you and get ready to enjoy some family time but most importantly, think about all the reasons you are grateful for. If you want to build your list of gratitude’s, let your imagination fly and begin to decorate with the help of your loved ones.
