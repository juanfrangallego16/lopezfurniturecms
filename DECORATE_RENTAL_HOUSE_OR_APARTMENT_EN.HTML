HOW TO DECORATE A RENTED HOUSE OR APARTMENT

When we rent a house or apartment, it is natural instinct to begin making some chnages to feel more comfortable and at ease in the place that will become our home for some time.
However, it is important that we are clear about what type of changes we can make without violating the rules established by the owner. Itâ€™s good to be aware that these adjustments are coupled to our budget for our temporary home.
In this article, I want to be a guide so you can decorate your house or apartment with a personal and charming touch, turning it into a temporary home that you can enjoy with your family.

1.SELECT BEAUTIFUL TEXTILES

There is no doubt that textiles can become our best allies when defining the decoration in your home. If you make a good selection of textiles, you can be sure that you will obtain good results in decorating your home.

A good idea for your sofa is to place accent pillows or blankets in color or with the pattern of your choice. Make sure it goes according to the style you want to give to your home. If you prefer, you can also choose to change the covers on your accent pillows.

If you like curtains, you can also play with colors, design, and even textures. Also, let me tell you that curtains are perfect for hiding blemishes on your windows, if you have any.

Rugs are also perfect for any added decoration. They add a cozy and pleasant touch to the home.

Just keep in mind that the textiles you select are to your liking and they fit perfectly with the rest of the decoration in your home.

The important thing is that all the textiles you choose for a room match each other.

2.GIVE YOUR WALLS A NEW LOOK

The walls have several options to give them a new look. One option is to paint them with colors of your choice. If painting is not an option, make use of vinyl to give style to your decor or even place some pictures that complement the decoration of your house or apartment.

All the adjustments you make will depend on your budget and the changes that the owner allows you to make. If the landlord allows you to paint the walls, that's already a big plus; if not, don't worry. You can use vinyl that simulates wallpaper or think of other alternatives such as woven tapestries, baskets, or mirrors.

If you are not allowed to make holes in the walls, you can use adhesive strips to hold them (only recommend not very heavy pieces).

3.PLACE PERSONALIZED DECORATION

A very nice detail is to add decorations or souvenirs that tell a story. If we analyze it well, this can be one of the most effective details to create that sense of belonging in the spaces and of course it will create that feeling that you are at home.

Be detailed, take the time you need to look at the corners and thus determine where you can place a decorative piece. You can give your home a personal touch without cluttering the place with random items.

In the event that you want to buy new pieces, I recommend you always look for quality and practicality. It is convenient to have pieces that are easy to install and move, remember that you will take them with you when you have to move.

4.GOOD LIGHTING IS NECESSARY

Having good lighting creates the feeling of a more pleasant, cheerful, and even healthy space. If you can, try to keep the windows clear during the day so that natural light can enter. During the evening, illuminate the spaces with lamps.

Natural light is very important as it favors the well-being of those who live in the home. Also, take into account that good lighting will allow you to better appreciate the details in the decoration, such as colors, shapes, and finishes.

When selecting lamps, make sure they match the rest of the decor in your temporary home. If you are looking to achieve a relaxing and pleasant feel in the spaces of your home, it is very important that in addition to good lighting you keep it clean and tidy.

5.SELECT SOME PLANTS

Plants have the power to transform any space in your home, I have no doubt about that. In addition, plants go well with all styles of decor, since you can find them in different sizes and for all likings. Just figure out which plants are the right plants for your home.

I recommend you go buy plants you already have a desegnated spot for in your home. This way it is easier to choose a plant that adapts to your environment.

I hope these ideas are insightful. I invite you to put them into practice so that you can create pleasant spaces that make you feel at home. If there are things that you cannot change in the house or apartment your renting, don't worry. Accept the rules and use your creativity by using other elements that help you create that home that you want so much.

My final words to you are these, to feel comfortable in a house or apartment you do not need to appear on the title deed (ownership). The really important thing is to decide that this will be YOUR temporary space and that you will do everything possible to take care of it and enjoy it for as long as you have.
