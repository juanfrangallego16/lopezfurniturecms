GOOD IDEAS FOR YOUR KITCHEN

If you dream of a beautiful decoration or remodeling your kitchen, you don't have to worry. For you to achieve a great result you don't need to have a big budget. You could apply small changes and, in this way, give a twist to the old decor in your kitchen.
It is very important that you make the correct selection of each of the elements that will be a part of this space in your home. You may also need to get rid of some of the old or outdated items in the kitchen.
Don't know where to start? Well, no need to panic about not knowing. My recommendation would be that you start with the basics, which in this case could be painting the walls. You could start by choosing the color palette that will bring your kitchen to life and get to work painting this area of your home.
If the furniture you currently have in the kitchen is already very old, then itâ€™s time to change it. Based on your budget, prioritize which would require an immediate change and replace that with new furniture.
The wonderful thing about remodeling spaces in the home is that you can give it the style of your choice and of course give your decoration a personal touch.
Perhaps for many, making changes in the home can be stressful. For others it can be fun. The important thing about this whole process is the result, it is when you realize that it was worth all the effort made to give your family comfort and convenience in all areas of your home.
Sometimes only small changes are required such as removing or changing some elements or adding some pieces to the decor.
An important point is that if you are tired of the flooring you have in the kitchen, you can consider changing it too. You can place some kitchen rugs/mats that will help you create the feeling of a different space.
If your kitchen is spacious and you have a free wall, you could add a painting to the decoration, in this way you will give a touch of art and beauty to this space.
Without further ado, today I want to share some ideas so that you can decorate your kitchen in an easy and simple way, but that will undoubtedly help you to create that beautiful kitchen that you dreamed of so much to enjoy with your family.

1.COLORS FIRST

Unquestionably, changing the color palette of the kitchen, whether on the walls and in everything that integrates it, generates an automatic change, and even totally changes the atmosphere in the home.

So, at this point it is time to be creative to select the color palette that will harmonize with your kitchen. Of course, it is also valid to seek advice if at any time you need it.

For the selection of colors, I think it is also important that you consider your tastes and those of your family since it will be a space for family enjoyment, and it is important that they feel comfortable in that space.

Painting the entire wall of your kitchen will give it another look and even give you the feeling that you are in a new kitchen.

2.HOW ABOUT A BREAKFAST BAR OR ISLAND?

For some reason, many find it pleasant to eat food in the space where it was prepared. Who doesn't like to have breakfast in the kitchen? Either because it is much more practical not having to carry the dishes to the dining room or simply because it is much faster for those who are always in a hurry.

In this case, you can choose the option of your choice, an island, a counter, or add a breakfast bar to the kitchen where you and your family can enjoy delicious breakfasts.

3.ORGANIZATION IS IMPORTANT

A well-organized kitchen is the key to making this home space beautiful and pleasant. When the cabinets, the counter, or any corner of the kitchen is overflowing with items and utensils, it is certain that you will not have harmony in that space.

To enjoy the new cuisine, organization is very important. Therefore, I suggest you organize your drawers taking advantage of the different options on the market that will help you keep everything organized. In this way you can enjoy a pleasant and cozy kitchen.

4.WHY NOT? CHANGE HANDLES OR KNOBS

Due to constant use, handles and pulls tend to discolor and deteriorate over time. Yes, this point is more about renovating than decorating your kitchen. But if you decide to change these pieces, you will see a small but beautiful difference in your kitchen.

If you want a more drastic change, you can completely change the cabinets. Regardless of what you choose, the important thing is that you will see a change in your kitchen, in different proportions depending on your choice.

5.MAGNETIZE AND DECORATE YOUR KITCHEN

We all know the magnetic tapes that are used to store knives. But the truth is that we can apply these tapes anywhere in the kitchen. Apart from that not only the knives are magnetic, but also cans and decorative magnets. This will give your kitchen a bolder look.

Of course, it all depends on what style you want and if you like to play around with the decoration.


As you have seen, the options to give life and a different touch to your kitchen do not imply spending a fortune. It all depends on the proportions of the changes you want to make and of course it will also depend on the quality of the elements you use.

Let nothing limit you when giving any space in your home the attention it deserves. Do not leave any detail to drift. Consider the lighting, if necessary, acquire new ceiling or wall lamps for this space.

If you like plants like me, you can add some to the kitchen decor. This detail will add freshness. You can even look for some that are low maintenance in case you don't have enough time to take care of them.

It is not necessary that you integrate many elements to the decoration, only those that are necessary for the space. Do not forget that on many occasions "less is more." Remember that the kitchen is not only the place where we prepare our meals. It is also one of the family gathering points.

You can be sure that decorating your kitchen will be very entertaining. Without a doubt, this is one of the spaces in which we spend more time every day and to which we must dedicate due attention.

I hope these tips will help you to create the kitchen of your dreams. Once again, thank you very much for reading every week and I say goodbye until the next article.
