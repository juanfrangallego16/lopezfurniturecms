TYPES OF SOFAS FOR YOUR HOME

From my point of view, the sofa is undoubtedly one of the most important pieces of the living room because in it we spend part of our time sitting watching television, reading a book, talking, etc. I am sure that many like I, at some points have fallen asleep on the couch. So due to the importance of a sofa in the home, it is necessary to analyze several aspects to find the most comfortable, durable, and suiting sofa for your needs.
Yes, I know it is a difficult task, since there is a wide variety of sofas on the market. So, I recommend you to be clear about some points such as: your tastes, the use according to the needs you have at home, the space you have, and the number of family members. Believe me, being clear about these points will make the process easy.
I think that in the end what most of us are looking for is comfort. That is why it is a good idea to personally go to a furniture store and make the correct selection with the good advice I will provide. Something curious but very true is that we all have a different physical complexion, which is why we perceive comfort differently.
Today I have prepared for you a basic classification of some types of sofas that you will find in the market, with a brief description of their characteristics.

1.SINGLE SEATER - CHAIR

Sometimes, the choice of the sofa is carried out according to the functionality that it provides us. The chair or armchair is perfect in rooms with limited spaces.

It can be used to complement other sofas with more seats or to take better advantage of a corner in the living room. They are also ideal in bedrooms by creating a corner where you can relax or even read if reading is a form of relaxation for you.

Unquestionably, the chair or armchair is a good choice if adding one more seat when it is impossible to add a sofa or loveseat.

The chair is available in different modalities such as, the French armchair, the reclining armchair, among others. If a chair still doesn’t interest you, you can move it around whenever you need to. It’s an easy piece to relocate.

2.CHESTER OR CHESTERFIELD SOFA

These types of sofas, in addition to being beautiful, can adapt to various styles, adding a touch of sophistication and elegance to the space in which you place them. This style of sofa fits like a glove to the classic style and goes well with the vintage style too.

At first this type of sofa was upholstered with leather, but now you can find Chester sofas with velvet, chenille, among other materials and of course with different shades.

As you will notice, it is a classic sofa with curved arms and low backrest, upholstered with padding and a series of buttons distributed in a geometric way, which at the beginning of the 19th century served to set the mood for English living rooms.

It is also necessary to bear in mind that because it is robust and rigid, many people find it a bit uncomfortable.

3.SOFA BED

I am sure that this type of sofa was designed to provide a solution to the problems of space and functionality of small homes. Today, this piece of furniture has been considerably modernized, improving its comfort and its opening system.

In this sense, we can find Italian opening sofa beds, book-type opening, and convertible sofas that become beds by moving their cushions to the seating area, among others.

For many people, it can be as if we have an extra room. The truth is that it is a piece that has evolved over the years.

It is perfect for those who are concerned about living room space. If you are one of those who frequently receive visitors or relatives at home, a sofa bed would be of great help. Of course, take good note when choosing that it fulfills both functions without exclusion, and that once installed it does not obstruct doors or passageways.

4.SOFA WITH STORAGE CAPACITYS

In some homes the storage capacity is scarce. Like beds, sofas allow us to convert the space under their seats into an immense chest in which we can store objects that we do not usually use.

In homes with small spaces and with children, it is difficult to maintain order and have storage space. Fortunately, there are sofas on the market with a built-in storage space inside their seats or in the chaise lounge. This is perfect for storing blankets, pillows, and toys in a neat and practical way.

5.MODULAR SOFASS

There are some spaces in the home that, due to their distribution and their shape, make it difficult for us to make the proper selection of furniture and this in some cases can make us despair. The point is that in these cases the most viable option is modular sofas since they give you the ability to remove or move each module as many times as you need. In other words, you can increase or decrease the size of the sofa according to your needs.

It is a conjunction of pieces for creativity and dynamism as it has movable sections that can be adapted to any corner.

6.TWO-SEATER (LOVESEAT) OR THREE-SEATER (SOFA)S

The traditional two-seater (loveseats) or three-seater (sofas) are perfect because they adapt to any type of space. They are ideal for the living room, and you can even place them in the reception of an office.

It is unquestionably one of the most used options in homes because it does not take up a large space. If you have larger spaces, you can combine it with another sofa and with an armchair or some other type of seat that fits the style with which you have decorated your home. The sofa and loveseat set are the most popular in homes and offices due to the many options.
