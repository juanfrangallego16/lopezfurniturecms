MINIMALIST INTERIOR DECORATION

Minimalist interior design is much more than an interior design and decorating style. It is a safe route to creating a nuanced and warm home that is worth investing in.
Minimalist interior design is known all over the world. It takes the modern style as a reference and simplifies it even more. In this style, the neutral color palette, simple furniture, and straight lines are predominate. A key point is to be aware that in minimalism there can be nothing excessive or extravagant in decorative accessories.
Itâ€™s philosophical basis applied to design is understood by stating that it is more important to give priority to needs before anything aesthetic that does not have any functionality.
In short, "Do more with less." That is why in minimalist interior designs you will not see elements that are not designed for an obvious purpose.
What characterizes the minimalist style? Simplicity, I think there is no better way to describe this style.
The most difficult thing in a creative process like this is to eliminate the excess and keep what is needed.
If you know minimalist interior design very well, you know that it is more than what meets the eye. This style creates spaces where you can relax and think clearly.
Lovers of the minimalist style see the simple as something wonderful. Some may think that minimalist spaces are cold. For those who identify with minimalism, it is perceived as a simple and elegant style.
If you are a lover of minimalist interior design, we want to share with you three points that will help you when it comes to giving your home this charming style.

1.ORDER AND PROLIXITY

Without a doubt these are two of the aspects that stand out in minimalism, the spaces are quite fresh and discreet. Remember that the key is to use furniture that is very functional and has a minimalist contemporary style, with defined lines.

These spaces must be well lit with natural or artificial light. If we analyze it well, in a certain way they are spaces that have a very elegant character and that is why they can seem a bit cold in the sense that what is sought is sobriety and good taste.

Empty spaces are considered important in minimalist interior design, as space interacts with objects and defines it. Visual balance is just as important and can only be achieved by having a focal point.

Something that you should keep in mind if you want to give your home a minimalist style is that you should avoid placing many colors and textures. The important thing is to carefully coordinate your furniture and materials to achieve a good decoration.

So, if this is the style you want to achieve, you just have to get to work and plan a nice design. If what you want is to renovate your home and give a total twist to those spaces that are already boring and old-fashioned, the minimalist interior design is an excellent option.

2.COLORS FOR A MINIMALIST DECORATION

Keep in mind that there are usually no more than three colors to decorate a house with in a minimalist style. In this style of decoration the colors tend to be very flat.

The color palette in a minimalist decoration is characterized by being very sober and neutral. The basic colors are white, black and red, using gray to decorate on other occasions.

Black and white are used to create space. Red is to create emphasis in specific places.

Another effective combination is that of black and white combined with wood and metals. In addition to these materials, smooth plastics are materials that are very abundant in the minimalist style.

3.FURNITURE AND ACCESSORIES FOR MINIMALIST DECORATION

Minimalist design enhances elegance and simplicity in spaces. Therefore, minimalist furniture does the same. When decorating with minimalist furniture, the basic volumes are applied fundamentally: minimal shapes, the cube, the sphere, the elemental polyhedra. We will tell you about it so that you can identify them more accurately.

There are not many pieces a part of the minimalist decoration. Perhaps a vase or a simple centerpiece. Although rugs are essential for this type of decoration, but nothing more than that. Keep in mind that functionality is the essence of what is done in minimalist interiors, which is why having space to store everything we have is essential.

Small details add to the depth of the minimalist decor. Streamlined joinery, flat doors, bedrooms without a headboard, or a simple wood panel headboard.

With all that said, don't think it's a cold style at all. It is simply another way of seeing things. Minimalist decoration is a style that is more for the essentials and for those who enjoy this way of seeing things.

To conclude, remember that "less is more" is the essence of minimalist decoration, focused on the essential and a commitment to simplicity, leaving aside all those elements that can end up overloading a space. The minimalist trend arises as a result of the traditional Japanese style and stands out for reducing the presence of furniture or decor in the home, giving a greater prominence to the space.
