7 IDEAS TO MAKE YOUR BEDROOM LOOK LIKE A LUXURY HOTEL

The rooms in a luxury hotel are synonymous with relaxation, rest, and comfort. Would you like to bring that comfort to your bedroom? I want to share with you these ideas that will give a touch of elegance and distinction to any bedroom.
When we arrive at a luxury hotel room, many of us lie face up on the bed, which is why the first thing we look closely at is the ceiling. Decorating it will completely change the look of your room. A beautiful idea is to add moldings. There are various very modern curvilinear designs that also include lighting details to create suggestive and pleasant environments.
In this article, I will be sharing some tips on how to turn your bedroom into a luxurious hotel room. You can rest assured that the moment you make the changes you'll be ready for the best nights of your life. Dare to replicate the cozy atmosphere of a high-end hotel with these simple but effective decorating ideas for your bedroom.

1.A BED DRESSED IN LAYERS

The leading role in a luxurious hotel room has to be the bed, most of the time. An excellent idea is to dress it in layers with a sheet, top sheet, blanket, quilt or duvet, some pillows, decorative cushions, and blankets for a sophisticated touch.

You may have noticed that a key element of hotels is the use of white sheets. These convey the feeling of peace, cleanliness, and relaxation, in addition to bringing freshness to any room. You can play a little with the color in the comforter or duvet, but always keep in mind that they must match the rest of the decoration in your bedroom.

I also want to mention and address the comfortable mattresses of excellent quality most hotels have. A good quality mattress is the avenue to the best night sleep. Lay down a new set of freshly laundered and pressed sheets. Take your time to place them well. A trick seen in hotels is that after placing the fitted sheet, place the top sheet, leaving about 20 inches hanging from the top of the bed. Then place the blanket, comforter, or duvet on top. The end of the sheet that is reserved on top can be used to make the upper envelope. You proceed to adjust all the ends and voila! Remember that a nice bed is always covered with a few cushions and the pillows you use to sleep on.

2.NIGHTSTANDS

The nightstands or bedside tables are infallible. It is not only decorative, but also functional. One mistake some people make is overloading their nightstands with unnecessary items and end up with a messy table.

Nightstands don't need much. They are perfect for placing small lamps and storing those objects that we always want to have in hand at bedtime. In short, they are tables that offer us not only aesthetics but also comfort.

3.SELECT A NEUTRAL COLOR PALETTE

It is true that most luxury hotels use neutral colors in the rooms, because this provides a sense of tranquility, good taste, and good rest.

Getting the right selection of colors for the room is very important to achieve the goal we are looking towards. I can even say that the color of the room is paramount. Keep in mind that neutral colors convey tranquility and serenity, and it should be noted that the place where you can feel this in a more tangible way is in a bedroom.

The neutral color palette goes beyond the walls. You will find that the curtains and upholstery also complement each other perfectly to create a calm space where you can rest after a long day.

4.LUXURY DETAILS

It's not just the bedding and décor that makes a five-star hotel feel so luxurious. It’s also all the little details that are a part of the bedroom. For the decorative elements you can add mirrors with frames, paintings, or sculptures. The key is to infuse the atmosphere with harmony through the decor, without over saturating the space. I think the secret is in the details. Anything that makes you happy and improves your mood deserves a place in your room, whether it's art or decoration. Surround yourself with things that make you smile.

5.LIGHTING

The lamps on the nightstands are must. There is no doubt, they are indisputably necessary. A viable option would be to choose strategically placed recessed lights to illuminate areas of your bedroom. Remember that the light must be soft and warm.

On the other hand, hanging lamps will give a more luxurious look to your bedroom. Another option is to place a dimmer so you can adapt your lights to any occasion.

6.CURTAINS AND RUGS

Curtains are essential in hotels to block light in the room and thus provide a restful sleep. A good idea is to place two sets of curtains, an internal one with a fabric that prevents the passage of sunlight and an external one that serves as accent to the rest of your decor. You can even leave some leftover fabric to bunch up on the floor.

Modify the appearance of your bedroom by incorporating a rug. This point is a way to give a cozy touch to the room as well as giving you a soft place to step on each morning and night. There is a wide variety of rugs for you to get crazy with your selection.

7.THINK OUTSIDE THE BOX

Sometimes all you need is a change of scenery to make your bedroom feel more luxurious. You can achieve this by changing the arrangement of the furniture in your room. Think outside the box, you can try placing the bed in front of a window. Who knows? It can work! Or even placing it at an angle. The important thing is not to keep your ideas hidden, but instead put them into practice and try different options. This will bring you closer to reaching your goal.


As I mentioned before, luxury hotel rooms are designed to provide you with a space where you can relax and enjoy a good night's sleep while away from home.

They definitely prioritize comfort with items like freshly laundered bedding, fluffy pillows, and soft furnishings, in addition to a nice decoration. These elements combine to promote your relaxation, help you to dissipate the stress of travel, and this makes the room a pleasant destination.

You can replicate the same relaxing environment in your own room without the expense of expensive travel. The trick is to place a variety of luxurious items, such as bedding, pillows, and rugs, to make the space as inviting as possible. Fresh scents and proper arrangement of bedroom furniture also help create a five-star hotel feel.

Implement these ideas in your bedroom or even if you have a guest room you can apply them there, to give a first-class treatment. Once again, thank you very much for reading and see you in the next article.
