SIMPLE IDEAS TO DECORATE YOUR HOME IN AUTUMN

Autumn is undoubtedly my favorite season of the year. It is the season that inspires us to create the most beautiful and cozy decorations.

If you haven't already, it's time to look at all the rooms in your home and start planning to make them warm and welcoming. There are many changes that you can apply to give that autumnal touch to within your home, with details such as changing furniture pieces, accent pieces, among others. These changes can help you create a more meaningful autumn feel at this time of year.

Many of us love when temperatures drop, because it is the ideal time to change the home decor and create much warmer and perfect environments to enjoy in the company of our friends and family.

Take advantage of this fall season to give your home the new look you deserve. It is time to make small but significant changes in our homes. Time to make changes in home decor that you can apply and that will give you the warmth you need.

1. THE PERFECT TIME FOR RUGS

Autumn is the time of year when earth colors are common. Therefore, it is an excellent time to completely change the decoration of your home.

It is the ideal time to dust the rugs softer and warmer, which are wonderful to accompany us on the cold days that are about to arrive.

There is a wide variety of styles and colors, but we can’t deny that neutral tones will continue to take lead. This season, rugs will become one of your best allies to bring warmth to your home. Unquestionably they are decorative elements, which, although they may not seem like it, can make a difference in any space.

Always keep in mind that rugs are one of the pieces that will bring the most warmth to your home in the fall and winter months. They give you a warm feeling beneath your feet that you look forward to when finally making it home after a long day of work.

2. WHAT DO YOU THINK OF FURNITURE WITH CURVED EDGES?

There is no doubt that this fall, the design of curved and organic lines continues to be a hit. It will give a different look to your decoration, in addition to giving it dynamism. But above all it will counter the straight lines of the sofa or any other piece in the living room.

They are very visual pieces, and they also turn out to be very pleasing to the eye. So, don't think too much about it and dare to include decorative elements with curved lines that add style to the decoration of your favorite spaces.

3. A COZY READING CORNER

This is without a doubt one of my favorite points. When autumn arrives, many of us love to spend quality time at home. Thinking about it, we could say that it is an ideal season for a gathering in your home. For this reason, I think that every living room needs a beautiful chair or recliner. In addition to being comfortable, it is perfect for creating the perfect reading corner.

If you are a book lover, you will agree with me that creating a cozy corner where you can read or relax is a plus. You don't need much, just a nice and comfortable seat, place some blankets, plaids, and some decorative elements that integrate perfectly with the decoration.

My recommendation is that you opt for comfortable textures, as well as warm colors, such as brown, and combine them with beige or white, to create a less dark environment.

I’m sure by now you have noticed it is a simple idea to start, and it does not require large financial outlays either. Do you think it's a good upgrade to enjoy this fall?

4. WOODEN DETAILS

From my point of view, accessories and decorative pieces are like the icing on the cake. In this point, wooden sculptures add style and character to any decoration.

Wood is always a great ally in decorating your home, due to its quality and versatility. In addition, we cannot deny that it combines perfectly with the autumn tones and brings warmth, elegance, and simplicity to any space.

Similarly, with the arrival of cold, wood is essential to decorate the home. Wood is present in the most refined and sober lines of the Nordic or Japanese style, but also in the wabi sabi style and in the cozy and evocative rustic style. You can apply this point by placing wooden furniture in the different spaces of your home.

5. PAY ATTENTION TO TEXTURES

In this season, velvet fabrics are very common. You can have it on the sofa, the armchair, or in any detail that decorates your space. You can even use it in your bedroom. Textures are the key to see the evident change in your decor each and every season.

If you are one of those who loves curtains, I must say that this is the season to change those vaporous curtains, or those made of fine fabrics, for ones made of slightly thicker and warmer fabrics. Change the throw pillows with fabrics such as velvet, wool, or any thick fabric that will give warmth to your home.

When autumn arrives there are many details that you can consider when decorating. When it starts to get cool, the warmth that a beautiful fireplace offers is the perfect addition to helping create a cozy atmosphere. It will also add style to your home.

You can complement the decoration with a vase or pot. Plants and flowers are an essential element in this season's decoration and will not go unnoticed, seeking to attract attention in terms of textures and designs.

If there is something that completely changes the aesthetics of the room, it is the pieces that add personality. One of them can be a coffee table or even an accent cabinet.

The fall of the leaves brings with it autumnal colors inside the home and out. The natural woods that bring us closer to the dense forests of large ancient trees also set the trend.

The goal this season is to make our home a little warmer and create cozy environments that invite us to spend a rainy afternoon in our home. Let our bedroom become an oasis for rest. Easy tips? A good use of color and decorative accessories will transform your home with the most comfortable and enveloping autumnal atmosphere.

Put some of these tips into practice and enjoy wonderful moments in harmony with your family. Thank you very much for reading and I say goodbye until the next article.
