READY FOR DECEMBER? BRING THE MAGIC OF CHRISTMAS TIME TO YOUR HOME

Some of the most important dates and of course the most anticipated of the year are approaching, so it is time to think about how to bring the magic and fantasy of this season to our home. The month of December has some of the most special celebrations of the year.
This month is full of symbols and spirituality, it also allows us to meet with our loved ones and there is no doubt that this is more than enough reason to have the home in optimal conditions to welcome them.
When December arrives, all the spaces in our home can be filled with beautiful Christmas decorations. I am sure that preparing the home for the upcoming celebrations will provide us with a placid, festive, and pleasant atmosphere at home, in which lights and colors are the protagonists and as well our originality, sweetness, and charm.
The decoration with the characteristics of this season is a relevant factor when creating a cozy atmosphere at home. So, in December, do not skip out on the Christmas decorations and the lights.
Yes, it is true that when December arrives there are also changes in the home, but they are changes that are pleasant, dynamic, and temporary, which will allow us to enjoy the comfort, joy, and magic of the holiday season.
Before starting to decorate, ask yourself what feelings you want to convey with your decor. Think about the style and the colors you want to apply. Take the time to walk around your home and visualize the spaces that you would like to decorate, in this way you can have a clearer idea of which ones you would like to highlight.
Today I want to take a short tour of some points that will help you decorate your home with beautiful Christmas decoration. My recommendation is that you pay attention to the following points so that you begin to decorate and fill the spaces of your home with magic this holiday season.

1.A CHRISTMAS TREE

Unquestionably, the Christmas tree is one of the star elements in any Christmas decoration. And nowadays everyone applies it in their own way. Some examples would be wall Christmas trees, white, green, natural, artificial, among others.

From my point of view, if you truly want a magical Christmas, your home needs a tree that meets expectations. Whether natural or artificial, the important thing is that it is the right size for the space you have. If space is limited, lean towards a small tree. If, on the contrary, do not skimp! You will see the light brough into the living room.

The good thing about Christmas trees is that you find them in different sizes, depending on the needs of each home.

My recommendation so that you try placing it in different spaces of the living room until you find the one that convinces you the most. Likewise, every year you should renew the decorations with the colors and motifs that go with the season.

2.ANY CORNER IN THE HOME DESERVES A CHRISTMAS TOUCH

If you are like me, you take the opportunity to fill your home with Christmas decorations, which completely change the atmosphere at home.

Go ahead and decorate not only the living room, take advantage of each space, the hall, the corridor, the dining room, the kitchen, if you have stairs at home, you can also decorate them. You will see how every detail adds up to make your Christmas magical.

Christmas wreaths are elements that can help decorate doors and walls. There are wreaths of different styles that will fit perfectly with the Christmas decorations that we have in our home.

If you have a fireplace in your living room, make it the second star of your Christmas decorations. In addition to some Christmas garlands, add some advent stars, some candles and some stockings in the most traditional style.

3.TAKE ADVANTAGE OF THE MAGIC OF LIGHTING YOUR CHRISTMAS

When December arrives one of the most used are the lights, either on the Christmas tree, in the garlands, or in any corner that you want to highlight these Christmas holidays.

You can find a wide variety of options with colored lights, with specific shapes, among others. The ideal is to choose the lights that best suit our decoration, and, above all… NOT OVER DO IT.

Placing lights on the tree, on the mantel, on the bookshelf, or decorating the wall will completely change the look of your decoration. You will see how the room is lit up with hope and joy.

Another option to illuminate your home is candles. They will help you create a warm atmosphere. Candles are the perfect complement to Christmas decorations.

4.DON'T FORGET TO DECORATE THE DINING ROOM

The dining room becomes one of the centerpieces of Christmas celebrations. This is where the whole family gathers for the lunches and dinners typical of these holidays. Therefore, its decoration should be appropriate for the occasion.

The dining room table and chairs are the most important pieces of furniture in the room and as such they deserve all the attention when it comes to decorating for this season.

Beautiful tableware, tablecloth, or a Christmas table runner with a beautiful centerpiece will be essential to creating the ideal dining space to spend your holidays in.

The purpose of all the decoration for this season is that you can enjoy pleasant moments with your family. We hope you enjoyed today’s article and we wish you and your family a Merry Christmas.
