CHILD AND PET APPROVED SOFAS

On many occasions the sofa becomes the protagonist in the decoration of the living room. In fact, the pieces that complement the decoration of the living room are chosen considering the model of sofa that has been selected to decorate our living room.
The choice of sofa is very important and much more so when there are children or pets at home. Based on this, the selection criteria is broadened because it is necessary to consider the practicality and easy maintenance of the selected sofa.
It is true that having pets at home is a very pleasant experience since they are good companions. Pets are part of the family and are almost always around the house.
At home we have three beautiful dogs that we rescued from the street and took them in temporarily while we found them a home. I can say that they don't leave home anymore, they have become part of the family and although at first it was difficult to adapt because they used to love to climb on the sofa. They still do today, but they don't try to destroy them anymore, they just like to lay on them.
Having children and pets at home has its benefits: they improve empathy, fill our hearts with joy, and help us stay more active. But the advantages are reduced when we focus on the relationship with decoration. Keeping the house impeccable becomes complicated.
Today I bring you some important points that you should consider when choosing a sofa for your home if you have children or pets at home.

1.RESISTANT FABRICS

My recommendation is that you opt for a sofa set with fabric that is easy to clean and keep in optimal condition. Remember that on the sofa you will spend pleasant moments sharing with your family, including pets, so opt for an upholstery that is firmly sewn and has a smooth surface, as they are easier to clean in case the children spill something on the furniture. But of course, they are the most durable against pet claws.

You can opt for a tightly woven fabric. At the same time, it is also important that it has a percentage of synthetic fiber, which makes it easier to clean, it does not shrink, and does not fade.

With children it is more reasonable to opt for a fabric sofa, instead of a leather one; although, this option should not be ruled out. Leather is easy to clean, it is also elegant and perfect for decorating any room.

Of course, you will have to take care of scratches and small scratches as they can leave marks, but with a good treatment to remove and treat scratches, everything will be solved.
In addition to natural fabrics, also consider synthetic ones, such as microfibers, which are generally solid, not dirty, and sometimes can even be put in the washing machine.

2.VELVET “NOT A GOOD IDEA”

Although velvet is in trend, the truth is that it is not highly recommended for a home with children and much less with pets, since it is somewhat delicate, allows stains to penetrate and is difficult to clean.

But if you like that texture, opt for smooth chenille’s, which imitate it very well and can survive a children's party at home.

They are beautiful and elegant. But in a home with children and pets it is not the most suitable. If liquids fall on it or your cat digs in its nail, it will be a total disaster. Better use washable fabric sofas in dark colors, such as brown or medium gray.

3.SOFA WITH WASHABLE COVERS

The models with covers allow you to wash cushions and backrests when you need it. Of course, first check that the fabric contains polyester and be careful with cotton and linen, which can shrink.

It is a wonderful option to buy sofas with washable covers, since the covers can be removed and washed as many times as you want; in the washing machine, by hand or be sent to dry cleaning, if the fabric is very delicate.

You can also opt for a quilted cover that goes over the seat cushion and allows children or your pet not to be in direct contact with the upholstered sofa. In this way, we will avoid several headaches by simply having to remove the sofa cover to wash it, without further complications.

4.SOFAS WITH DARK COLORS AND A FIRM STRUCTURE

Go for darker colors, avoid white as it can tarnish easier. Another option is the intermediate shades, so that they do not mark the decoration excessively.

Just as important as its upholstery and padding is the frame. Those made of solid wood, steel, or aluminum are good options. If you want to know if it is resistant or not, raise one of its legs 15 cm from the ground and check that the other leg is stable, and the sofa does not tilt.

Yes, you have small children and pets, you must adapt your lifestyle to the reality of your home. That is why you will not always be able to incorporate certain innovations in the sector into your decoration because they do not fit with the functionality you need. This does not mean that you have a mess in your home. No! It's just about striking a balance between aesthetics and practicality.

With these tricks, the coexistence between decoration, children and pets will be more placid. In the end, these "sacrifices" are well worth it.

And if you cannot resist some of these trends, you know: to take extreme precautions and that is what God wants.

Remember, choosing the materials well and seeking a balance between the aesthetic and the practical are the keys to a happy coexistence.
