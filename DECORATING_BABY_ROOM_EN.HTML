DECORATING THE BABY ROOM

If you are about to welcome a new member into the family, take a deep breath and don't panic because you will need to make some changes in your home. You’ll need to prepare the room for him/her, and I am sure that at this point you have many questions. What to do with the walls? Do you paint it, or do you use wallpaper, among many other questions that may arise?
Organizing the room for the baby requires time, because there are many details that must be considered, such as the gender of the baby since this will help you when selecting the furniture, accessories, and the color palette for your decor.
In the baby’s room, the details are very important since they will help you create a pleasant and cozy environment.
Think of the nursery as the babies first window to the world. For this reason, it is important to add some visual, tactile, and sound stimuli to the decoration. Add music, stuffed animals, and rattles so that as he/she grows, they can explore the world through these objects. There are many decorative accessories with colors and sounds that will stimulate the baby's senses.
Here are some points that will help you make the right decision when decorating the room for the new member of your family.

1.COLORS

From my point of view, the room for a baby should be cozy and very pleasant so that the little one can rest, play, and feel stimulated by their environment.

In the decoration of the baby's room, white and pastel colors should not be lacking, although; it can also be decorated with neutral tones without losing an iota of sweetness.

You can incorporate the brightest colors in some accessories, toys or any other object that will help you visually stimulate the baby’s senses, without abusing them since the little one also needs to rest, and it is not a secret that colors influence their mood.

A very important point to keep in mind is that the paint you use should be ecological or water-based, so that it does not release toxins into the environment.

2.WALLS

When it comes to the walls, you should stay away from the colors that are very strident. Remember that the purpose of the decoration for the baby's room is to provide good rest.

You could choose neutral colors that do not exhaust you over time. If you choose to wallpaper it, timeless patterns such as florals or botanical details can work perfectly, always in soft tones of course. The wallpaper gives personality to the decoration.

Some other wallpaper options could be drawings of clouds, birds, raindrops, and many more. The truth is that your options are endless. I can assure you that any of these details are worth it, since the result will be a sweeter, more magical, and more beautiful room.

One way to add color to the nursery is by creating a focal wall. Just keep in mind that the color adapts to the decoration and the amount of natural light in the space.

If you have an artistic streak, you can create a mural with paint for the prominent wall of the room. Find a theme that you like and use colors according to the decoration to integrate it perfectly.

3.FURNITURE

In this, the leading role during the first months is played by the crib. Remember that it must provide safety and comfort to the baby. From the presence of bars to a protector that surrounds the crib, there are many details that as parents we cannot ignore during this first stage.

Selecting an armoire to complement the decoration of the room is essential. Using some auxiliary furniture with wheels could also work for you.

It would also be a good option to place a complete wardrobe from floor to ceiling since with this you will have more storage space, and we must not forget that the baby will grow quickly and as time progresses you will see the need to make small changes to the room so that your little one has a beautiful space suitable for each of his/her stages.

A seriously recommend you focus on light colors like white. Believe me this never fails when it comes to decorating the baby's room.

To have all the baby's things in perfect order, you can help yourself with all kinds of resources, from the classic armoire to some baskets, minimalist drawers, and multipurpose cabinets in which you can store their clothes, diapers, hygiene products, toilet, amongst others.

I encourage you to add a single chair to the decoration. There are so many chair options that I am sure you’ll be able to find one that goes well with the decoration in the babies bedroom.

4.RUGS

Rugs are important accessories for the decoration of the baby's room. It will add warmth and texture to the space, it can be very helpful in the stage in which the little one begins to play on the floor.

You could use a decorative fluffy rug throughout the space and on top add some didactic mat that combines well with the decoration. I can assure you that your baby will thank you.

To protect the health of your baby, you should not skimp on expenses when it comes to textures. Choose organic cotton for the clothes in his/her crib and decorate with natural fiber rugs where he/she can sit and play safely. You can place several cushions around to make it more comfortable.

5.LESS IS MORE

Your little one should be able to have enough space to start developing their imagination during playtime. Think carefully about what furniture and what elements you are going to add to the décor. It is better not to load the room too much because, as our little one grows, he/she will accumulate toys and items.

I sincerely hope that these little tips help you create the ideal room so that you can welcome the new member of your family. And if you do it with love, I am sure that you will create the dream space for your baby.

It’s always fun to decorate the room for the baby with the help of the family. This can be fun and do not forget to give your personal touch to the decoration while you wait for the arrival of this new little person in your home.
