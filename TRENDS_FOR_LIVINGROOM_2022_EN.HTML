TRENDS FOR THE LIVING ROOM IN 2022

With the passage of time, new trends in interior decoration emerge, which means that many of us make small or large changes in our home from time to time.

It is true that trends change and evolve, but it is also true that they do so at different rates. Some are popular and last over time, others only for a few seasons.

An example of this could be trends like Nordic, minimalist, classic style, among others since they have been around for years and yet continue to surprise and delight us over time.

We have started a new year and I want to share with you some trends in interior decoration for this 2022.

1.ELEGANT AND TRADITIONALG

We know that elegant and traditional never goes out of style, which is why this type of decoration continues to be a trend among living room styles. To achieve this style in your home, it is best to choose spacious furniture and elegant elements. With colorful elements such as vases, chandeliers, and sculptures.

In this type of style, it is important to combine the colors correctly, mainly using light and neutral tones, such as beige and white. It's also a good idea to choose accessories with a metallic look, as well as items that are a bit ostentatious, such as crystal pendants, large curtains in neutral colors, mirrors, and portraits that showcase some of your family's most special moments.

2.ABSTRACT DESIGN

It is one of the funniest trends. It is inspired by abstract expressionist art. In this trend we can see bold geometries, hand-drawn sketches, and even fun blocks of color.

The purpose of this style is to evoke emotions and create spaces with positive and cheerful environments. Bold patterns and vibrant colors provide a fun approach to living room decor.

Much like the expressionist art movement, this trend celebrates the imperfection and fluidity of hand-drawn shapes. Line drawings play a key focus within this style.

3.SIMPLE AND WARM

If you are a lover of the trends of calm, comfortable and relaxed spaces. There are several options that can work perfectly, it's just about making our homes a safe and welcoming space to rest and regenerate.

Create a quiet yet uplifting space to disconnect from the outside world. In this you can even apply the trend of being conscious of sustainability. If you like a simple, understated, and understated style, but still want a home that feels relaxed and comfortable rather than overly minimalist, you could combine it with a craftsmanship style, natural materials, and unrefined finishes. Textures and tactile qualities are replacing bold colors to add interest.

It is this type of decoration I would recommend adopting warm neutral tones, such as oatmeal and natural suede. Beige returns as the perfect base color to build on.

4.CONTEMPORARY

To create contemporary living room styles, it is important that you select rich colors combined with more subtle elements. For example, you could keep the walls a light color and choose furniture in terracotta tones or even patterns. The most important thing is to experiment with shapes and colors to create a fresh and modern space.

Among these types of styles, minimalist touches can also be used, with elements of straight lines and soft colors that provide decorative details to the space. It is also possible to create a combination of styles, adding modernity through the range of colors, elegance through the lamps, and simplicity with subtle pieces of art.

5.ECLECTIC GLAMOR

There is no doubt that this trend reinvents the sophisticated elegance of the thirties with a little opulence in the style of the seventies.

Furniture with curves and flowing shapes take center stage in this elegant style. Think of the elegant chairs of the 1920s with shell-shaped backs and smooth edges.

Dare to choose rich hues for key pieces, such as royal blue or ruby, mixed with warmer tones of ochre, pink and bronze. Apply metallic touches, think shimmering gold, copper, and bronze.

This trend is perfect for those who want to show off their quirky and glamorous side in the living room. Velvet is coming in strong for the coming seasons, especially when it comes to furniture.

Investing in a velvet sofa is a great way to pull off the look. However, if you're not ready to take that step, opt for a velvet cocktail chair or a glamorous light fixture.

6.JAPANDI

There are many elements of Japanese design that have been present in the interior collections during the last few seasons. Several decor and furniture homes have presented us with collections full of rich silk textures, elegant bird motifs, stylized oriental prints, and structured furniture shapes.

A mix of elegant Japanese minimalism and rustic Scandinavian simplicity, Japandi is a hybrid trend that brings together the best of these two beloved styles. This trend is for you if your home is your sanctuary and you want a less-is-more approach with uncluttered spaces, clean lines, and a calm, subdued color palette.

Choosing quality over quantity will give small spaces a feeling of spaciousness. While more intricate patterns and bold dark pieces work just as well in larger rooms.

Soft lighting, like this bamboo floor lamp, helps add soft ambient light that will always keep homes calm and serene.

7.RETRO

Another of the most fun and creative room styles is retro decoration. This is one of the styles that has generated the most trend and will continue to do so in the coming years. To achieve a retro room, the first thing you should do is acquire retro-style furniture, with contrasting colors to the rest of the space.

It is important that you complement the style with many colorful accessories that allude to a particular era, such as radios or old trunks. As well as shelves, clocks, and even fun details like telephones from the 60s.
